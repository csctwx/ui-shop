International Rates

Connect to friends and family around the world with our low per-minute international rates. Sprint Prepaid lets you say goodbye to those confusing calling cards and talk on your terms.

How it works

  • International calls are charged at the international per-minute rate for the country you’re calling plus your standard airtime rate.
  • Calls to Puerto Rico, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands and the U.S. Virgin Islands are included in Sprint Prepaid’s standard airtime rates. There is no additional international charge for these locations.
  • International text messages cost $0.15 each to send and receive.
Available countries and/or calling rates are subject to change without notice. Please visit this page regularly for changes including, but not limited to, included countries, rates and fees. Sprint Prepaid plans do not require an annual contract. No plan discounts apply. Price and offers subject to change. While on the Nationwide Sprint Network, the availability of international calling and/or texting services may depend on your plan. If available, additional charges will apply and be subject to the rates noted above. All features not available on all phones. Plans are for domestic use only within the U.S. unless stated otherwise noted and include Caller ID, Voicemail, Call Waiting, Three-way Calling and Call Forwarding while on the Nationwide Sprint Network. Usage limitations apply.

Mobile Hotspot

Turn your phone into a mobile Wi-Fi® hotspot and share your phone’s data connection with up to 5 wireless devices. Laptops, tablets, gaming consoles, and more. It’s Wi-Fi on the go.

Mobile Hotspot is already preloaded on most Sprint Prepaid phones and there is no extra charge. So you can start sharing right away.

Sharing your Mobile Hotspot is really simple. Just open "Settings" on your phone and follow the directions. Easy.

Mobile Hotspot available only with select devices/plans. Connectivity dependent upon compatibility and speeds vary. When hotspot ‘on’, all data usage (phone and hotspot) counts against data allowance; turn off hotspot when not connected to other devices.
