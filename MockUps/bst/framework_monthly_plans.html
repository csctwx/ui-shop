More Information

How to maintain your account

Add money to your account any time to keep your account on and uninterrupted.

Make sure to add sufficient funds in your account to cover your daily subscription amount. The daily charge will be deducted automatically from your Boost Mobile account after midnight (based on your time zone) each day, regardless of usage.

Recharge at least once every 90 days to keep your account active (after 90 days any credit balance will expire). Once you hit a zero credit balance, you have 60 days to add money and keep your service and account active.

How to add money to you account

Adding money to your account is easy. Choose the method that works best for you.

Dial #ADD (#233) to add money from your handset or visit My Account online. You can also securely register your preferred way to pay online to make faster one-time payments anytime.

You can also pay in person with cash or credit at one of thousands of authorized retail locations.

How to use Auto Re-Boost™

Once you've registered a credit/debit card or account, you can set up automatic payments. It's called Auto Re-Boost™ and it's the easiest way for you to keep your service active.

Sign up for Auto Re-Boost™, and Boost® will automatically process your monthly payment every month on your monthly payment date.

How to check your balance

Checking your balance is quick and painless.

Dial #BAL (#225) from your handset to check your balance for FREE.

You can also check your monthly payment date (for Monthly customers) and add funds to your account with your registered way to pay.

Phone Insurance

Can't live without your cell phone? Get Phone Insurance so you don't have to. Here are some reasons to choose Phone Insurance:

  • Without insurance, you may be required to pay full price for a new phone if a replacement is needed.
  • Insuring your phone provides you peace of mind by protecting your investment in the event of loss, theft, accidental damage, and out- of-warranty mechanical or electrical breakdown.
  • Select smartphones get the added protection of Gadget Guardian™ powered by Lookout®. This app allows you to locate, lock or wipe your smartphone if it becomes lost or stolen.
  • It's easy and convenient.
  • You can typically receive a replacement phone as early as the next business day, once your claim is approved.

How does it work?

Simply enroll in Phone Insurance when activating your new phone or in My Account within 30 days of purchasing your new Boost Mobile® phone with an accompanying monthly plan.

You are eligible for 2 approved claims within a 12-month period beginning on the date of the first replacement. Per claim limit is a maximum of $1,500 inclusive of accessories.

Once your claim is approved, you will pay a nonrefundable deductible of $20, $50, $100, or $175 depending on your phone. See list of phones and their applicable deductibles.

If you cancel between monthly billing cycles, you will receive a prorated refund or credit for any unearned premium.

Your phone will be replaced with a reconditioned one of like kind and quality. In the event a reconditioned phone is not available, the replacement will be a new phone of like kind and quality.

Phone Insurance is a replacement service provided to customers of Boost Mobile. This coverage is underwritten by the Zurich American Insurance Company. eSecuritel Agency, LLC is the licensed agent and eSecuritel Holdings, LLC administers the program.
