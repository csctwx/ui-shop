Connect to friends and family around the world with our low per-minute international rates. Sprint Prepaid lets you say goodbye to those confusing calling cards and talk on your terms.

    How it works:
  • International calls are charged at the international per-minute rate for the country you’re calling when you’re using your monthly plan talk minutes. Standard airtime minute rates may apply for minutes over any monthly talk minute allotment.
  • Calls to Puerto Rico, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands and the U.S. Virgin Islands are included in Sprint Prepaid’s domestic monthly talk plans. There is no international charge for these locations.
  • International text messages cost $0.15 each to send and receive.

Available countries and/or calling rates are subject to change without notice.
