Turn your phone into a mobile Wi-Fi® hotspot and share your phone’s data connection with up to 5 wireless devices. Laptops, tablets, gaming consoles, and more. It’s Wi-Fi on the go.

Mobile Hotspot is already preloaded on most Sprint Prepaid phones and there is no extra charge. So you can start sharing right away.

Sharing your Mobile Hotspot is really simple. Just open 'Settings' on your phone and follow the directions. Easy.

Mobile Hotspot available only with select devices/plans. Connectivity dependent upon compatibility and speeds vary. When hotspot ‘on’, all data usage (phone and hotspot) counts against data allowance; turn off hotspot when not connected to other devices.
