Tips on international calling with Sprint Prepaid

  • Make international calls the same way you would from any other phone. Dial 011 + the country code + the city code + the phone number. For countries like Canada, Dominican Republic, Jamaica, and other countries marked with a “*” in the table, you don’t have to dial 011 before the number
  • International calls are charged at the international per-minute rate for the country you’re calling when you’re using your monthly plan talk minutes. Standard airtime minute rates may apply for minutes over any monthly talk minute allotment.
  • Calls to Puerto Rico, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands, American Samoa and the U.S. Virgin Islands are included in Sprint Prepaid’s domestic monthly talk plans, so – there is no international charge.
  • International Text: $0.15 per international SMS message sent and received.
