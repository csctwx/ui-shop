What is Sprint Prepaid? How does it work?

With Sprint Prepaid there are no annual contracts, no credit checks, surprises or late fees. There are also no compromises with unlimited talk and text plans with varied data options and some of the latest technology including 4G LTE and Sprint Spark capable devices. You simply 1) choose a compatible Sprint Prepaid phone, 2) select a plan based on your phone and how much data you want to use per month and 3) activate and prepay each month for your service.

Can I keep my existing phone number when I switch to Sprint Prepaid?

In most instances, yes. However, porting your wireless number from certain carriers may not be available or limited, depending on your carrier. Call Customer Care at 1-866-866-7509 for more info.

If I already have a Sprint device, can I use it with a Sprint Prepaid plan?

Sprint Prepaid devices are specially designed to work with Sprint Prepaid plans. Sprint Prepaid plans are limited only to approved Sprint Prepaid devices.

Can I combine my Sprint Prepaid account or payment with another Sprint account?

At this time, there is only one person or subscriber allowed with each Sprint Prepaid account. While we try to make it simple to manage your Sprint Prepaid account at any time through My Account , Sprint Prepaid accounts cannot be combined with Sprint postpaid accounts.
