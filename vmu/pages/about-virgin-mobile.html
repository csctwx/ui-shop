The Virgin Mobile Story

About Us

Virgin Mobile USA has been a consumer champion in the no-contract wireless industry since 2002 - we believe you deserve flexibility, choice and an all-around fair deal that delivers to all of your expectations.

So jump on board with Virgin Mobile and find what you've been looking for: great value without being locked into a long-term contract, advanced devices, reliable coverage on the nationwide Sprint® network and maybe a little fun along the way.

  • Virgin Mobile Unlimited Plans - Do unlimited your way - choose the phone you want and the plan you need, on a nationwide network without a contract. Virgin Mobile gives you the flexibility and choice with new wireless plans starting at $20 per month. Visit www.virginmobileusa.com/cell-phone-plans/data-messaging-plans/overview/ for more information.
  • Broadband2Go - Take nationwide 3G and 4G LTE (where available) mobile broadband service on-the-go for as little as $5 a day or $24 a month. Data whenever, wherever. Visit www.virginmobileusa.com/mobile-broadband-plans/broadband-2-go/overview/ for more information.
  • payLo - payLo by Virgin Mobile was especially designed with value-conscious wireless consumers in mind, offering plans for as little as $20 per month for 400 minutes. To learn more visit www.virginmobileusa.com/cell-phone-plans/paylo-plans/overview/

Company Overview

Virgin Mobile USA, one of Sprint's prepaid brands, offers customers control, flexibility and connectivity through no-contract unlimited wireless plans on the Nationwide Sprint Network. Virgin Mobile offers a selection of quality handsets ranging from entry-level to Android™-powered and iOS smartphones available at more than 40,000 retail stores throughout the country, including Target, Walmart, Best Buy, RadioShack and hundreds of convenience and electronics stores.

Top-Up cards are available at approximately 150,000 locations Experience Virgin Mobile on the Web at Facebook, Twitter, You Tube and purchase Virgin Mobile USA products at www.virginmobileusa.com .

  • Virgin Mobile gives you the flexibility and choice you deserve so you can live life on-the-go.
