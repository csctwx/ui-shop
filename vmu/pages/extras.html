Visual Voicemail

Comes preloaded on your Android smartphone and allows you to listen to voicemails in any order.

Why You Need It

Why call in to your voicemail box to get your messages? With Visual Voicemail*, you don't have to sit through numerous voicemails-you can see who left a message and pick the one you want to play back.

And for an enhanced user experience, upgrade your VVM client to enable Avatars so you can record and send audio voice mail messages with a visual Avatar that lip-syncs your message (i.e., monster, cat, zombie, etc.). Or, select a background to animate your message. You can manage, subscribe and cancel the new avatar feature via an added menu option in your Visual Voicemail application. Free backgrounds / avatars available, and you can purchase premium items (like photo backgrounds) through the VVM content store.

You can also go premium and subscribe to Visual Voicemail-to-Text for just an additional $2.99/month added to the cost of your monthly plan to receive transcripts of what messages are in your voicemail inbox. The premium subscription provides inline playback and auto-forward-to-email options. You'll also be able to use your own gallery pictures as backgrounds with your Avatar messages.

*Visual Voicemail is available on select Android and Windows phones only. Please refer to your phone's user manual for availability.

How To Enroll

Refer to your device manual for instructions on how to set up Visual Voicemail.

For Visual Voicemail-to-Text, open the VVM client and navigate to Settings > Manage Subscriptions. From here, you can subscribe to (and remove) Visual Voicemail-to-Text.

Text to Landline

Enables you to send a text message to landline numbers.

Why You Need It

With Text to Landline, your message will be delivered as a voice call and read to the person who answers the phone. If no one picks up, your text message will be stored as a voicemail, provided the recipient has voicemail service on their landline phone. First message is free of charge, and you'll be asked to opt-in. Once you opt-in, subsequent messages will be charged at $0.25. Subsequent message delivery will fail unless you opt-in to the service.

How To Enroll

Opt-in from your phone. All you have to do is send a text to a landline. Your first message will be delivered and you will get an opt-in request to be able to send future messages.
