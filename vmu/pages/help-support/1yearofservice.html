Programs & Policies

1 Year of Service

Virgin Mobile's 1 Year of Service will keep your Pay As You Go service active for a whole year. That means there's no need to worry about topping-up to keep your service from expiring. Just top-up whenever you need more airtime, or money for texting, ringtones, etc.

To participate in Virgin Mobile's 1 Year of Service, you must be on a Pay As You Go plan and add $80 or more in one payment transaction by using a credit/debit card or registered PayPal account.

When your year is up, you can choose to re-enroll in 1 Year of Service by adding $80 or more in one payment transaction. Otherwise, you'll have to Top-Up according to the Top-Up rules on your PAYGO plan to remain active and keep your phone number.
