ACCOUNTS, MONTHLY PLANS AND PAYMENT FOR SERVICES


Custom Account & Monthly Plan Setup. All Custom service offers require the creation of a Custom Account ("Account") and selection of a Custom Monthly Plan ("Monthly Plan"). A Monthly Plan consists of any combination of voice minute, text, and/or data service monthly allotments - as well as any add-on services (or "Special Offers"), if any, added to your Monthly Plan. In addition, a monthly line fee or other associated fees will be required for each device added to your Account.


Monthly Plan Cost and Payment. The total cost of your Monthly Plan may vary each month, depending but not limited to: the applicable service allotments selected; added Special Offers, if any; the number of devices on your Account; and any applicable sales tax and fees. State and local taxes may apply when adding funds to your Account. Before using Custom services, you must first pay-in-full for the cost of your Monthly Plan before you can utilize the services. Payment is made by funds from your Account, which are added through one of the approved Custom payment methods [credit/debit or Custom replenishment (aka "Top-Up") cards]. Adding funds through Custom customer service may result in processing fees., Note: A maximum of $200 can be added to your Account per transaction, or until your Account balance reaches or exceeds $400. Funds from your Account cannot be transferred between other Virgin Mobile wireless accounts and cannot be used to pay for other Virgin Mobile wireless services.


Monthly Plan Billing Cycle. Payment for your Monthly Plan is recurring and will be due on your monthly billing cycle date which is determined by the calendar date that the first Custom device was activated on your Account ("Cycle Start Date"). Your Cycle Start Date for your Account will be the same calendar date for all subsequent months; unless, activation occurs on the last calendar date, which will result in your Cycle Start Date being the last calendar date for all subsequent months. For example, if activation occurred on March 31st, your Cycle Start Date for the next month would be April 30th and the last calendar date for all subsequent months thereafter (i.e., May 31st, June 30th, etc.). Subsequent monthly billing cycles will automatically renew on the Cycle Start Date of each subsequent month, with Monthly Plan costs collected from your Account at the end of the day at 11:59pm, Pacific Standard Time, of the Cycle Start Date.


Payment and Services: Sufficient funds that cover the total cost of: your Monthly Plan service allotments, applicable Line Fees, any recurring Special Offers, if any, and any applicable taxes and fees, should be added to your Account before your Cycle Start Date in order to keep your Account current and services active. If insufficient funds are in your Account by the Cycle Start Date, your Account will be suspended and your service interrupted (excluding calls to care and 911) and you will lose any unused Monthly Plan voice, SMS and/or data service allotments. You will have 120 days after your Account/service is suspended/interrupted to add sufficient funds to your Account to prevent cancellation, loss of all remaining funds in your Account balance (if any), and your telephone number. Lost funds are nonrefundable. In order to bring your Account in good standing you must pay for the missed payment of your Monthly Plan (if any), Line Fee(s), and recurring Special Offers that were due at the time of suspension. When you bring your Account current, your Cycle Start Date will change for all subsequent months to the calendar date you brought your Account current and in good standing. If payment for your Monthly Plan fails, your services may be suspended until successful payment is made. All purchases and changes to your plan, like upgrades or downgrades, require your account to be in good standing and without failed payments pending. Your account is required to be in good standing in order to purchase new plans, manage devices, or modify your Monthly Services.


Device Line Fee. Each device added to your Account requires payment of a monthly "Line Fee". If a device is added to your Account on a date that is not on your Cycle Start Date (i.e., mid-billing cycle), the Line Fee cost may be prorated based on the days until your next Cycle Start Date. Conversely, no Line Fee credits or refunds will be provided if a device is removed from your Account before your Cycle Start Date. Line Fee service benefits, if any (i.e allotment of minutes, messages or data services) that are included with a Line Fee are applicable only to the first device added to the Account. Adding additional devices and Line Fee payments on your Account will not result in additional Line Fee service benefits. However, additional devices on the Account may share applicable Line Fee services, if any.


Monthly Plan Upgrades and Downgrades: You may elect to increase or decrease your Monthly Plan services by increasing ("Upgrade") or decreasing ("Downgrade") your Monthly Plan service allotments at any time directly on your Custom device(s), or on the Custom website through 'My Account'. Your Upgrade or Downgrade of Monthly Plan services will be effective immediately. Upon any change, you may receive a prorated Account credit for Downgrade of services (except for Line Fees) or, pay the cost difference between the former and new Monthly Plan allotment Upgrade of services. If your Monthly Plan is in suspended status for any reason, you will not be able to Upgrade or Downgrade your Monthly Plan Services, nor, will you be able to add any Special Offers to your Monthly Plan until your Account is current.


Account Termination. If a successful payment has not been made within 120 days of when your Account was suspended, your Account will be terminated and all active Monthly Plan services (including Special Offers) and connected devices, including their associated telephone numbers, will be removed from your Account. In addition, any unused services and remaining funds in your Account, if any, will be forfeited. If you wish to restart services following an Account termination, you may be subject to a reactivation fee, in addition to selection of a new Monthly Plan, in order to return your Account to good standing. Even if you later bring your Account into good standing after an Account termination, we may not be able to provide you with the same telephone number previously associated with your devices prior to your Account termination.


SERVICES & OTHER TERMS


Text Messaging Services ("SMS"): There is no guarantee SMS will be received. In addition, Virgin Mobile is not responsible for any lost or misdirected SMS. SMS will be deducted from your account's applicable SMS allotment for both inbound and outbound SMS. For SMS sent to multiple recipients, each SMS sent to a recipient counts against, and will be deducted from, your applicable SMS Account allotment. A single SMS consists of up to 160 characters. If a SMS is longer than160 characters (sent or received), an additional SMS will be deducted from the account's SMS allotment for any additional characters beyond the 160 SMS character limit.


NOTE: Multimedia Messaging Services ("MMS" or "Picture Mail") are not included as part of SMS services. The ability to send and receive MMS requires a Monthly Plan with data services. Utilizing MMS services will count against a customer's data usage allocation, if any. If Monthly Plan does not have data services, MMS will not be sent, nor will any be received.


Data Usage: Data usage includes but is not limited to, web browsing, downloading content (i.e., applications, games, music, video, etc.), sending and receiving MMS messages, ringtone and other application usage (including applications operating in the background), and other similar activities. Data usage is measured in one megabyte ("MB") increments, with each data session rounded up to the nearest MB. Data usage may depend on a variety of factors, including the size of the downloaded content, files, images, or applications. In addition, some applications may continue using a device's data connection in the background, even if the application is not being actively used or the application 'closed'. Such usage counts against your account's applicable data allotment.


Directory Assistance. Unless otherwise noted, calls made to directory assistance, operator-assisted calls, or pay-per-call services are not available with Custom Monthly Plans. In addition, "premium messaging" (i.e., donations via SMS or paid premium content) are not available.


Call Waiting. Call waiting services are charged at standard airtime minutes rates and count against your account's applicable minute usage allotment for each call while the connected parties remain on the line.


Three-way Calling and Call Forwarding - UNAVAILABLE: Three-way calling and call forwarding services are not available at this time.


Mobile Hotspot on Phones: Mobile hotspot is available only with select phones and plans. Hotspot data usage for phones will be the combined phone and hotspot usage, if applicable. Connectivity dependent upon compatibility and speeds may vary.


GPS Navigation: GPS-service require GPS-capable phone. Environment may limit GPS location information.


Local Phone Numbers. Some local phone numbers may not be available in certain markets. Additional fees may apply for phone number or equipment change requests (e.g., changing to a new phone) processed by customer service.


Monthly Plan Add-Ons (or "Special Offers") are available in addition to your Monthly Plan services and are provided on either: (1) a one-time, single-event purchase; or, (2) a monthly recurring additional service that is added to your Monthly Plan/Account and remain on your Account until removed. Purchase of Special Offers may be prorated if purchased before your Cycle Start Date. However, cancellation/removal of Special Offers from your Account will not result in refunds. The cost of selected Special Offers will be added to the total cost of your Monthly Plan and must be paid before use. Special Offers will be provided on a set allotment of usage and/or on a set time (or "term") limit use, in accordance with and subject to, the terms of this Agreement.


International Calling Plan Special Offers. International Calling Plan Special Offers provide you the ability to make calls to select international locations. Any attempt of outbound calls count against your selected allowance, if any, as specified in the respective International Calling Plan Special Offer. Each call will be rounded up to the nearest minute. Inbound incoming calls from international locations to your phone count against and reduce your existing voice services allotment. For purpose of clarity, using your device from an international location is not supported.
