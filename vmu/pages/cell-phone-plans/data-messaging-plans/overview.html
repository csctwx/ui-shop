Hassle-Free Switching

Keep Your Phone Number

Once you purchase your new Virgin Mobile phone, you can switch your number from another company to Virgin Mobile when you activate your phone.

No Contract

All of our plans are contract free. No commitments!

Free Shipping

All phones purchased from our website come with free FedEx Ground Shipping. Order by 11 am eastern time today and you'll have it in your hands in 3-5 business days. (Limit 3 phones per order.). Need it faster? 1-2 Business Day shipping is just $12.00. Just buying accessories? Ground shipping for accessory purchases is just $6.99.

7-Day Guarantee

Whatever the reason, if you bought a phone from us online, you can return it for a full refund within 7 days. Make sure you have the original receipt and packaging. If you bought a phone at a store, it's up to them whether or not you can return it. So check the store's policy.

Free Activation

Just grab a phone and go. No credit checks or activation fees.

Lost Phone Replacement

If your phone is lost or stolen, let us know right away so we can suspend your service. We can usually get you a replacement in just a few days, at a really low price. And we'll transfer any remaining balance in your account to your replacement phone.
