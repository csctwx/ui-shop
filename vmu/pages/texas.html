LIDA Credit for Texas Residents

Are you eligible for a monthly credit?

Texas residents may be eligible to receive a $9.50 monthly credit in their Virgin Mobile accounts based on criteria set forth by the Texas Low-Income Discount Administrator (LIDA).

Eligibility for the credits will be determined monthly by the state of Texas based on your enrollment in one of the following programs:

  • Food Stamps (SNAP)
  • Medicaid
  • Health Benefit Coverage under Child Health Plan (CHIP)


If you qualify in a given month, Virgin Mobile will send you a confirmation text message and the $9.50 credit will automatically be added to your account.



Frequently Asked Questions

How will the $9.50 be added to my account?
If you qualify in a given month, Virgin Mobile will automatically add the $9.50 to your account.

How will I know if I've qualified?
Virgin Mobile will send you a text message letting you know that the $9.50 was added to your account.

What can I use my $9.50 credit towards?
You can use your $9.50 credit any way you want. Put it towards your monthly charge or use it for minutes, text messages, downloads, etc.

Who should I contact if I have questions regarding Telephone or Electric discounts in Texas?
You can call LIDA at 1-866-454-8387.
