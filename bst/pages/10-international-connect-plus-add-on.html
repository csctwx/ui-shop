What does the International Connect Plus plan add-on include?

  • 1,000 talk minutes to mobile phones in Mexico! After the 1,000 mobile minute monthly allotment is depleted, rates will be $0.05 for every minute thereafter for the remainder of the monthly plan cycle.
  • Unlimited calling to ALL landlines in Mexico!
  • Reduced mobile and landlines rates to more than 200 international destinations including Dominican Republic.
  • Unlimited calling to over 70 countries, including Canada, Brazil, Venezuela, Colombia, Peru (landlines only in select cities).
  • Unlimited International text messages to over 200 international destinations worldwide from the U.S., Puerto Rico and U.S. Virgin Islands.
(top)

Does the International Connect Plus plan Add-On include international calls?

Yes, to select countries and cities. Click here to view the included countries and cities. Click here for current International Calling Rates to other countries and cities.

(top)

Does the $10 International Connect Plus plan Add-On include unlimited calling to Mexico?

The $10 International Connect Plus Add-On continues to include unlimited calling to ALL landlines to Mexico landlines as well as unlimited text messaging.

The International Connect Plus Add-On also includes 1,000 minutes to mobile phones in Mexico, with each additional minute thereafter charged at $0.05 a minute.

(top)

What happens if I exceed my 1,000 minutes of calling to mobile devices in Mexico?

Once you use your entire 1,000 minute allotment to mobile phones in Mexico, you will be charged a rate of $0.05 for every minute after for the remainder of your monthly plan cycle. Any additional calling charges incurred will be deducted from available funds in your Boost Mobile account balance immediately. If there are insufficient funds available to cover the costs of the additional calling minutes, calls to mobile devices in Mexico, as well as other international destinations not included as unlimited, will be blocked until sufficient money is added to the account balance to cover the incurred charges, OR until the next successful monthly payment is completed.

International Minute Packs, which include a Mexico calling option, are available to supplement your international calling needs. Click here to find out more information.

(top)

How can I track my usage of Mexico mobile minutes?

You will receive a text warning notification once you ever reach 75% (750 minutes) of your 1,000 monthly mobile minute allotment. As an alternative self-service method, you may also track your usage online in My Account.

(top)

How do I sign up for the International Connect Plus plan Add-On?

The $10 International Connect Plus Add-On is only available for Boost monthly plans. If you are a Boost monthly plan customer, you can sign up for the $10 International Connect Plus Add-On any time during the month via My Account or your local, authorized dealer. The Add-On features and service will NOT be available until AFTER your next monthly payment date, when you pay for the Add-On with your new monthly plan cost.

REMEMBER, adding the Add-On will increase the TOTAL amount due for your monthly services (monthly plan cost + add-on cost). Also, this is a recurring plan add-on; you must pay for it each month and will remain attached to your monthly plan and account until you remove it.

(top)

Where can I use the included services?

You can use the included services from the Boost domestic coverage area. See coverage maps for details.

(top)

How and when do I need to pay for the Add-On?

The International Connect Add-On is a recurring monthly plan add-on, with the cost being added to the cost of your monthly plan services. The new total plan cost must be paid at the same time you pay your monthly plan services and is due on your monthly payment due date. Your account balance must cover both the cost of your monthly plan services AND the cost of the International Connect Plus Add-On (and other plan add-ons, if applicable) by your monthly payment due date; otherwise, your services will be interrupted until sufficient funds covering the total costs of your plan and additional services (i.e., applicable add-ons, if any), are added to your Account.

(top)

If I have not subscribed to International Connect Plus Add-On, can I still make international calls and send international text messages?

Yes. However, you will be charged accordingly on a per-minute and per-message basis. Check International Rates often for the latest details and rates for international calls.
