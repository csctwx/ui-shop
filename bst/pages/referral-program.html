What is the Referral Program?

The Referral Program rewards customers for referring friends and family to sign up for an eligible Boost plan. See details.

(top)

How can I participate in the Referral Program?

Eligible monthly plan customers who meet the program requirements, as defined below, can participate in the Referral Program.

(top)

What do eligible Boost Mobile customers get for referring friends and family to Boost Mobile?

Eligible Boost Mobile customers will receive a $25 referral credit for each referral (up to $500 referral rewards per calendar year) who successfully activates on a monthly plan and meets the Referral Program requirements as set forth below.

(top)

What are the Referral Program requirements?

Existing Boost Customers

  • Sign up for the program and send referral offer to a friend
  • Have made 1 successful monthly payment in the lifetime of the account
  • Be currently on an monthly plan and in good standing at time of credit
  • Friend activates phone on an monthly plan has 1 successful monthly payment

Referral Boost Customers

  • Sign up for the program and receive referral offer
  • Perform a new activation within 90 days of receiving referral via any channel
  • Have made 1 successful monthly payment
  • Be currently on an monthly plan and in good standing at time of credit
(top)

When will this program end?

There currently is no end date, but Boost reserves the right to terminate and/or modify this offer at any time without prior notice.

(top)

What happened to the old Referral Program?

We expired our old referral program to help support the launch of our new program and to provide a better customer experience. The new program does not require a referral code nor activation onto a referral plan. Learn more about our referral program.

(top)

How many referrals can a Boost Mobile customer make?

  • You can earn a max of $500 referral rewards per calendar year. The Referral rewards annual limit begins with the date you were issued your first credit.
  • Referees, who get their first reward for being referred, are eligible to earn up to $475 per calendar year in additional rewards for referring friends too.
(top)

Who can I refer?

You can refer friends and family who are not currently Boost Mobile customers.

(top)

When will the $25 credit be applied to my account?

Please allow for up to 4 weeks to receive the $25 credit. Please check back at the referral program to view your referral and credit status. If you still have questions you can email the referral program care by signing in and selecting Contact Us.

(top)

What if I change my Mobile Number after I register for the Referral Program?

If you change your mobile number you will need to have the new number updated on your member account to continue to be eligible for Referral Rewards. Contact the Referral team at the website where you signed up to update your profile.

(top)

What if the person I would like to refer already activated service?

You must first refer friends or family before they activate service.

(top)

The person I referred deleted the referral email or no longer has the social media posting. What should I do?

  • You can resend an email or re-post from the Referral Rewards website homepage. Be sure to sign in first.
  • Your referral can also visit the website and select Were You Referred? and then provide the email address to register.
(top)

Do referrals expire?

  • Yes. Referral emails and social media invitations are valid for 90 days.
  • You can check the status of these referrals in the Referral Statement, located on the Home page of the website.
(top)

How much money can I earn in the Boost Referral Rewards Program?

  • You can earn a max of $500 referral rewards per calendar year. Referral rewards begins with the date you were issued your first credit.
  • Referees, who get their first reward for being referred, are eligible to earn up to $475 per calendar year in additional rewards for referring friends too.
(top)

The Boost Referral Rewards website is not working/displaying properly, how can I fix this?

For an optimal experience, you may have to temporarily disable your anti-virus software or ad-blocker.

(top)

What if my question is not answered here?

Customers can go to the support section of the Referral program website (via Boost Mobile Zone or boostmobile.com/refer) and send an email to the vendor support team. The Referral program website will also allow you to view your referral and credit status. If you still have questions you can email the referral program care by signing in and selecting Contact Us.
