What's included in the airG VIP add-on?

This add-on will provide you access to the premium content in the airG ID Pack.

(top)

Is airG VIP available to all customers?

No, airG VIP is available only for customers on a monthly plan.

(top)

How do I sign up for airG VIP?

You can sign up for airG VIP anytime via My Account or directly from the AirG app (download from Google Play). Once you sign up for airG VIP, services will be available at the beginning of your next monthly payment data after you have successfully paid for your total combined monthly charge. Your total combined monthly charge is equal to your monthly plan cost + $5 airG VIP add-on + any additional services you subscribe to.

(top)

When do I need to pay?

You must pay for airG VIP at the same time you pay monthly payment amount, on your Monthly Payment Date. If your account balance cannot cover both the cost of your Monthly Plan and any additional add-ons on your Monthly Payment Date, your service will be interrupted.
