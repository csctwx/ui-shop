Dealer Opportunities

Become a Boost Mobile Dealer




Boost Mobile is one of Sprint’s award-winning prepaid brands, and we’re looking for energetic retailers to join our team.

We listen to what customers want in a prepaid wireless carrier, and we offer premium products and services a cut above the others. The nationwide Sprint® 4G LTE network, no annual contracts and unlimited plans starting at just $35 a month are among the values that make Boost stand out.

Our brand is growing – we now offer phones from flip to 4G LTE, including iPhone. We want you to grow with us. Check out our Select Retailer Program and our Authorized Retailer Program and see which one works for you.





About our Master Agents

We utilize the best distribution partners called "Master Agents." They have the responsibility to ensure all parties involved in the distribution of and sale of Sprint Prepaid Group's (SPG's) products and services use their best efforts to promote and maximize sales of product while upholding the integrity of all Sprint Prepaid brands.

Master Agents provide local market representation, help facilitate inventory management, provide operational training and support, verification systems and reporting, advertising/marketing solutions, and owner planning meetings to give you options to decide what is best for your business.



Sprint Prepaid Group - Select Retailer Program

What are the qualifications?

Owner must...

  • live within market area stores are located.
  • have two years branded retail management experience.
  • have willingness and desire to open a minimum of five stores within two years.
  • have a comprehensive business plan.
  • have the financial capacity to open multiple stores.
  • have $100,000 in liquid assets.
  • accept Sprint Prepaid Group's Branded Retail Acknowledgement Terms and Conditions.

What's the investment?

Investments and costs vary by market and store location, but here are typical initial investments.

Fixtures: $9,000
Ex. Counters, seating, shipping, installation, exterior signage

Equipment and leasehold: $4,000
Ex. POS systems, computers, paint, flooring, track lighting

Pre-opening operating costs: $7,000
Ex. Staffing, training, lease deposits, grand opening expenses

Working capital: $20,000

Average break-even after opening is six months.

Master Agents typically provide a line of credit for initial inventory investment. If not, initial inventory is approximately $7,000.

Interested in applying?

Apply Online

Approved applicants will undergo an interview process with:

  • A Master Agent (MA)/business partner of SPG



Why join the Select Retailer Program?

  • No franchise agreement and no fees. Being a select retailer gives a multi-store owner the opportunity to make a six-figure income.
  • Receive a fast return on investment with your low-cost initial investment.
  • Earn competitive compensation through activating new customers, in-store and credit/debit card customer payment, accessory sales and residual income from new customers you sign up.
  • Sell select newly released devices weeks before other dealers. You can also be the first in line to have inventory set aside for your store location.
  • Have access to high-quality fixtures and signage, demo devices and brand-approved promotional materials to give your store a best-in-class experience.

Sprint Prepaid Group - Authorized Retailer Program

What are the qualifications?

Owner must...

  • live within market area store is located.
  • have two years retail management experience.
  • accept Sprint Prepaid Group's Branded Retail Acknowledgement Terms and Conditions.

What's the investment?

Investments and costs vary by market and store location, but here are typical initial investments.

Fixtures: $2,000
Ex. Glass case fixture, cash desk

Equipment and leasehold: $2,000
Ex. Banners, signage, lightboxes computer terminals

Pre-opening operating costs: $3,000
Ex. Staff training, marketing, collateral, inventory

Interested in applying?

Apply Online

Approved applicants will be contacted by a Master Agent; a business partner of Sprint, who will provide additional details about our programs, inventory management and operational support.




Why join the Authorized Retailer Program?

  • Earn revenue through new handset device sales, accessory sales and residual compensation on all new activations done through the Sales Portal.
  • Get sales support through promotional contests, marketing opportunities and event participation from the Sprint Prepaid Team.
  • Sell a selection of quality handsets from LG, Motorola, Samsung, Kyocera and ZTE.
  • Have access to high-quality devices and brand-approved promotional materials.
