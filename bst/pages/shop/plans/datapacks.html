Add a data pack to your monthly plan.


With data packs, you're always ready for a data binge. Any time you need more 3G/4G data, just add a data pack so you can keep streaming, gaming and surfing at high speed. The data pack will give you some extra data for the month.

Add to My Account

Here's how it works:

  • Add extra high-speed data to your monthly plan via My Account.
  • A data pack is separate from the data included in your monthly service plan.
  • The data in the data pack add-on will be used before the high-speed data in your monthly service plan, should it be available.
  • Use your data pack within 30 days of purchase.
  • Data packs are a one-time purchase. If you use all the data in your data pack before the 30 days are up, you can purchase an additional data pack.
