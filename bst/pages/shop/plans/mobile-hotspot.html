Useful Tips to Enable Mobile Hotspot on Your Phone

Instructions may differ by device, please consult your user guide for exact instructions.

iOS
Access Settings
Click Cellular>Set Up Personal Hotspot>Enable>Wi-Fi Password>Done

Windows
Access Setting
Click Internet Sharing>On>Setup>Set Broadcast Name and Password

Android
Access Settings
Tap "Mobile Hotspot" (may also called Portable Wi-Fi Hotspot, Network Sharing)
Identify and edit, if desired, the Wi-fi Name and Password. These will be needed to connect other devices to your hotspot.
Tap Save
Toggle Mobile Hotspot to 'ON'

Optional Mobile Hotspot add-on allows device to provide Wi-Fi access for other Wi-Fi enabled devices. $3 Daily option provides 500MB/day; $5 & $10 Monthly options provide either 1GB/30 days or 2GB/30 days respectively. Hotspot service available until end of applicable term from purchase date or until data allotment depleted, whichever occurs first. Usage measured by total combined data use of each connected device. Connectivity dependent upon compatibility and speeds may vary.
