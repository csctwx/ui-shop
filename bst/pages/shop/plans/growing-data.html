New! Only Boost® grows your data plan.

Get 500MB of extra high-speed data after every 3 on-time payments. Up to 3GB after 18 on-time payments. So the longer you stay with Boost, the more high-speed data you'll have for streaming, sharing, gaming and more.

Plus, all monthly plans include unlimited talk & text and reliable service on the Nationwide Sprint® 4G LTE Network. With Boost, it's all about getting more of what you want.

Start growing your data plan:

  1. Sign up. New customers must activate on a  Monthly Plan with Growing Data. Existing customers can change plans here. (Customers who sign up for the $60 plan have unlimited high-speed data!)
  2. Make your on-time payments. On-time payments are made on or before the monthly payment date. For every 3 on-time payments, Boost will grow your data by 500MB - up to 3GB after 18 on-time payments. To make it really easy, enroll in Auto Re-Boost and your monthly payment amount will be automatically deducted from your credit or debit card on your monthly payment date.
  3. Start growing. Once enrolled in a Monthly Plan with Growing Data you will receive text messages and/or emails to notify you when you have reached a data milestone!

Worried about missing a payment? While it's best to make regular on-time payments each month, payments do not have to be consecutive. If you miss a payment you don't have to start over. The on-time payments made to the account will still be credited towards your next data milestone.
