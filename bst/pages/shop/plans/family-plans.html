Get unlimited talk and text plus up to 5GB of high-speed data for each line when you create a Boost Mobile Family Plan.

These Family Plans come with the flexibility you’d expect from Boost Mobile. Since everyone gets unlimited data, talk and text, you won’t have to worry about surprising overages or spats over sharing. And, as always, there are no annual contracts.


Start Adding Lines

Family Plans include:

  • Unlimited* data, talk & text for each line
  • Unlimited Music Streaming without data charges (select music partners)
  • Optional add-ons – like international calling, data packs, music, games and more**
  • Reliable service on the Nationwide Sprint® Network
  • Call waiting, voicemail and 3-way calling
  • Free incoming calls and SMS even if you can’t pay, up to 60 days. Learn more.
*Each line includes applicable monthly high-speed data allotment; once allotment depleted, speeds reduced to 2G speeds until plan cycle ends. Int’l services extra.
**Primary line responsible for full monthly payment amount including all recurring charges. Secondary lines responsible for add-on purchases not part of the monthly payment amount and ad-hoc charges.

Here's how it works:

  1. In My Account, switch your phone plan to a Family Plan.
  2. Pay one monthly charge for all of your lines.
  3. Invite others – family, friends, co-workers – to join your Family Plan through My Account. (Tip: You’ll need to have their Boost Mobile phone number to send an invitation!)
  4. Manage each line and approve any add-on services that are part of a recurring monthly payment plan.
  1. Log in to My Account.
  2. Accept the invite from the primary account holder. This person will be responsible for paying your future monthly charges.
  3. Switch to the compatible Family Plan and agree to the terms and conditions. (All family plan members must choose the same plan type).
  4. Enjoy unlimited data, talk and text plus 1.5GB or 5GB of high-speed data each month.
