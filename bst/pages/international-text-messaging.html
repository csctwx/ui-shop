Our coverage maps provide high level estimates of our coverage areas when using your device outdoors under optimal conditions. Coverage isn't available everywhere. Estimating wireless coverage and signal strength is not an exact science. There are gaps in coverage within our estimated coverage areas that, along with other factors both within and beyond our control (network problems, software, signal strength, your wireless device, structures, buildings, weather, geography, topography, etc.), will result in dropped and blocked connections, slower data speeds, or otherwise impact the quality of services. (top)

Can I send messages from the U.S. to other countries?

Yes you can as long as you're in a coverage area. International messaging rates vary by plan. Click here for details. For the list of eligible International destinations for text, click here.

(top)

Can I send messages while traveling outside the US?

Yes you can send and receive text messages from Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands. See coverage maps for details.

(top)

Can I receive a message from someone who is located outside the U.S.?

Yes. Standard rates apply.

(top)

How much does it cost to send an international text message using my Boost Mobile® phone?

Monthly Phone Plans: International text messages that are received are included in your plan, but are 10¢/message to send. However, if you have either the International Connect or International Connect Plus add-on, international text messaging is included, to send and receive. For details, click here.

(top)

Can I send/receive picture, audio or video messages internationally?

No, currently we just offer international text messaging.
