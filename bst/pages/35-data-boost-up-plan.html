What is the $35 Data Boost Up plan?

Beginning 05/05/2015, the $35 Data Boost Up plan which includes unlimited data, domestic talk, text, and includes 2.5GB of 3G/4G high-speed data for just $35 per month with Auto Re-Boost will no longer be available for customers. However, current customers can keep their $35 Data Boost Up plan for as long as they keep their Boost account active. Their plan terms and benefits will continue as usual. If their account cancels, they will not be able to resume their former $35 Data Boost Up plan.

(top)

What features were included in the $35 Data Boost Up plan?

The $35 Data Boost Up plan included unlimited data, domestic talk, text, and includes 2.5GB of 3G/4G high-speed data.

Additional services, digital purchases and add-ons are available for purchase but may incur additional charges to your account.

(top)

If I purchased the $35 Data Boost Up plan with 2.5GB of data and exceed the allotment, can I switch to a different plan that includes more high-speed data?

Yes, if you have a smartphone, you can switch to any of the plans that offer more high-speed data and your new plan will be effective at the beginning of your next monthly plan cycle. However, only the $35 Unlimited Monthly plan offers a $5 discount for enrolling in Auto Re-Boost.

If you have a basic phone (non-smartphone), you can only switch to the $45 Data Boost plan with 5GB of high speed data.

(top)

If I am an existing Boost customer, can I switch to a $35 Data Boost Up plan?

Beginning 05/05/2015, the $35 Data Boost Up plan will no longer be available to customers. However, current customers can keep their $35 Data Boost Up plan for as long as they keep their Boost account active. Their plan terms and benefits will continue as usual. If their account cancels, they will not be able to resume their former $35 Data Boost Up plan.

(top)

If I remove Auto Re-Boost from my account what happens to my $35 Data Boost Up plan?

If you un-enroll from Auto Re-Boost, your monthly payment plan will automatically increase to $45 on your next monthly payment date but continue to receive 2.5GBs of high speed 3G/4G data. You must pay the increased amount by your next billing date.

(top)

What happens to my $35 Data Boost Up plan if my credit card registered with Auto Re-Boost expires?

You will need to register a new card with Auto Re-Boost. Auto Re-Boost will only work with a valid, active registered credit or debit card.

(top)

What is Auto Re-Boost and how do I set it up?

Auto Re-Boost is Boost Mobile's automatic payment method which allows you to register a credit card or debit card with Boost Mobile®, which can be used to automatically make your monthly payment according to the type of plan you're on. Auto Re-Boost is the easiest way to make sure your account and monthly services stay current and active.

You can enroll in Monthly Auto Re-Boost which allows your monthly payment amount to be automatically debited on your monthly payment date from your credit/debit card that is registered with Boost®. Click here for more details about Auto Re-Boost.

(top)

What add-ons will be available with the $35 Data Boost Up plan?

Almost all add-ons (e.g., International Connect, International Minute Packs, Phone Insurance, etc.) are available for an additional purchase with the $35 Data Boost and Data Boost Up plans. Currently, the $5 Todo Mexico Plus add-on is limited to the plans starting at $45 or higher.

(top)

What phones can I use on these $35 Data Boost Up plans?

The majority of Boost phones are eligible, except the BlackBerry 8330.

(top)

What happens when my data speeds are reduced?

After your monthly plan data allotment of 2.5GB is reached, access to data continues but data speeds will be reduced to 2G speeds for remainder of your plan cycle. Speeds will be restored at the beginning of your next monthly plan cycle.

(top)

What happens if my data speeds are still reduced and I want another high-speed data allotment?

You need to wait until your next monthly payment date to get your high-speed data allotment renewed or you can use the Restart Plan option and make your full monthly payment early to re-enable all your services immediately by visiting My Account or calling Customer Care.

(top)

How can I see and manage my data usage?

You can view your data threshold, usage and status in My Account. You will also receive notifications as you get closer to your included data allotment of 2.5GB.

(top)

When is my monthly payment date?

Your monthly payment date is the same day of the month you made your first monthly payment. For example, if you paid your first month of service on February 15, then your monthly payment date is the 15th every month (March 15, April 15, etc.) and remains the same as long as you successfully make your monthly payment, on-time, every month. With Auto Re-Boost, you will not need to worry about it as long as your form of payment remains actively registered.

If your monthly payment date is the 28th, 29th, 30th or 31st of a month then it will always be the same day or the last day of the month.

(top)

Can I change my monthly payment date?

No, your monthly payment date remains the same every month as long as you successfully make your monthly payment on-time.

(top)

What happens if I miss my monthly payment?

If you are enrolled with Auto Re-Boost with an active form of payment, your service will not be interrupted.

If you un-enroll or remove Auto Re-Boost from your account and are on the $35 Data Boost Up 2.5GB plan, the plan cost will increase to $45 and be due by your next MRC date for continued service. You can restore the $35 price by re-enrolling in Auto Re-Boost. You will need to add $45 to your account to restore your service and re-enroll with Auto Re-Boost in order to get back on the $35 Data Boost Up plan.

Once service is restored, your monthly payment date will change to one day prior to the date that service was restored (e.g., if your service is restored on February 15th, your monthly payment date will be reset to March 14th). Restoring service at the end of or on the first of the month (i.e., 30th, 31st or 1st) will result in varied payment dates contingent on the last calendar day of the month.

You have 60 days to resume service by paying your monthly payment in full or your account will be cancelled and you will lose all funds in your account balance and your phone number.

(top)

What happens if my account balance goes to $0 before my next payment is due?

If your account balance reaches zero, but you've already enrolled in Auto Re-Boost, you will be able to use all the unlimited services included in your plan for the remainder of your monthly plan cycle and your registered form of payment will be charged for the following monthly recurring charges.
