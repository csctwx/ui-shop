(top)

What is the $30 Unlimited Plan with Growing Data?

The $30 Unlimited Plan with Growing Data is a limited time offer for customers who register for Auto Re-BoostSM on the $35/mo. Unlimited Plan with Growing Data to receive a $5 monthly discount. The plan provides the same benefits as our $35/mo plan including unlimited domestic talk, text, and data (which includes 2GB of high-speed 3G/4G data) for just $30 per month with Auto Re-Boost enrollment.

If you un-enroll in Auto Re-BoostSM, you will automatically pay $35 starting on your next MRC but any accumulation of data benefits you've earned will not be impacted. You must pay the increased plan cost on your next payment due date in order to continue your service.

(top)

If I'm currently on the $30 Data Boost Up plan, do I automatically get the new Growing Data benefits on the $30 Unlimited Plan?

No. You will need to do a plan change in My Account in order to get access to the $30 Unlimited Plan with Growing Data AND take advantage of the new $35/mo Unlimited Plan with Growing Data with all the benefits it offers. You will also have to re-enroll in Auto Re-BoostSM to get the $5 Discount. This is a great way to take advantage of two benefits offered by Boost Mobile!

(top)

Does the Growing Data benefit apply to the $30 Unlimited Plan?

Yes! It's a great way to take advantage of both offers! Boost is currently running an offer for customers who sign up for Auto Re-Boost on the $35 plans and will receive a $5 monthly discount. These plans are also eligible for the Growing Data rewards even if you enroll or un-enroll in Auto Re-Boost during the time you are on Growing Data. The plan cost is $35 without Auto Re-Boost activated on the account.

This is a great way to take advantage of two benefits offered by Boost Mobile!

Auto Re-Boost allows you to set up automatic payments with a registered credit/debit card which automatically charges you on-time each month on your payment due date. It's called Auto Re-BoostSM and it's the easiest way for you to keep your service active.

(top)

If I purchase the $30 Unlimited Plan with Growing Data plan with 2GB of data and exceed the allotment, can I switch to a different plan that includes more high-speed data?

Yes, you can switch to any of the plans that offer more high-speed data and your new plan will be effective at the beginning of your next monthly plan cycle. However, the $45 Unlimited with Growing Data plan for 5GB and the $60 Unlimited Monthly Boost plans- do not have a discount for enrolling in Auto Re-Boost.

(top)

What happens if I miss my monthly payment?

If you are enrolled with Auto Re-Boost with an active form of payment, your service will not be interrupted.

If you un-enroll or remove Auto Re-Boost from your account and are on the $30 Data Boost Up 2GB plan, the plan cost will increase to $35 and be due by your next MRC date for continued service. You can restore the $30 price by your next MRC date by re-enrolling in Auto Re-Boost. You will need to add $35 to your account to restore your service and re-enroll with Auto Re-Boost in order to get back on the $30 Data Boost Up plan.

Once service is restored, your monthly payment date will change to one day prior to the date that service was restored (e.g., if your service is restored on February 15th, your monthly payment date will be reset to March 14th). Restoring service at the end of or on the first of the month (i.e., 30th, 31st or 1st) will result in varied payment dates contingent on the last calendar day of the month.

You have 60 days to resume service by paying your monthly payment in full or your account will be cancelled and you will lose all funds in your account balance and your phone number.
