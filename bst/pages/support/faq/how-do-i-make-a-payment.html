How do I make a payment?

You have the option to pay with cash, credit card or debit card. And you can pay in person, online, or over the phone.

3 Ways to pay online:

  • Auto Re-BoostSM– Free (no charge):

    This is the easiest way to keep your service working without interruption. Set up Auto Re-Boost by logging in to My Account. Then click on 'Payments' and 'Enroll in Auto Re-Boost' from the menu on the right. Select Auto Re-Boost and enter your debit or credit card information.

  • Visit My Account to make a one-time payment – Free (no charge):

    Sign in to My Account and click on the 'Re-Boost Now' button. From there, you can use a debit or credit card to make a one-time payment or redeem a Re-Boost® card.

  • Add money to a friend or family member’s account – Free (no charge):

    Go to give a gift. You'll need your friend or family member's Boost Mobile phone number and a credit or debit card. You can instantly add $10 to $99 to their account.

4 Ways to pay from your Boost Mobile phone:

  • Pay through the Boost Zone app – Free (no charge):

    Open the Boost Zone app and click 'Pay Next Month.' Use your debit card, credit card or Re-Boost card to add funds.

  • Dial #ADD (#233) – Free (no charge):

    Dial #ADD and follow the voice prompts to pay with your credit/debit card or redeem a Re-Boost card. 

  • Text ADD to 7225 – Free (no charge):

    Redeem a Re-Boost card by texting ADD and the card's PIN to 7225. (For example, text ADD 12345678912345.) Make sure to enter a single space after ADD.

  • Text PAY to 7225 – Free (no charge):

    Text PAY, the dollar amount you want to pay, and the last four digits of your registered credit/debit card to 7225. (For example, text PAY 50 1234 to add $50 to your account with your registered card ending in 1234.) Then reply Y to confirm your payment.

Pay in person:

Use the Re-Boost Locator to find a retailer near you and pay by cash, with a credit/debit card or purchase a Re-Boost card.


Security Information:
For your security we recommend you delete the text messages after viewing, just in case you were to lose or have your phone stolen. We do not provide personal information with our text messaging service. For example, only the last 4 digits of your stored debit or credit card are sent by you to confirm payment. We also recommend password protecting your device to help prevent anyone else from accessing any information on your cell phone and is a convenient way to keep all your information on your device safe.
