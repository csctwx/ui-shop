Text Based eCare

Boost Mobile's Text Based eCare allows Boost customers to access account balance and make payments via Text. All Boost customers are eligible and there is no need to sign up or register your device. Please note that all transactions from a device will return information or add money to the account of the device being used.

Text Based eCare Instructions

English

Spanish

For English, send any of the commands below to 7225:

  • BAL: to receive account balance information
  • ADD(space)Re-Boost PIN# example: ADD 12345678912345
  • PAY(space)AMT(space)Last 4 digits of registered debit/credit card example: PAY 50 1234
  • HELP to receive information on available keywords

BAL or BALANCE for current account balance information

ADD(insert a space)Re-Boost PIN# on Re-Boost card or receipt to add the amount of your Re-Boost card to your account. Example: ADD 12345678912345. Make sure and only enter a single space after ADD. Verify your Re-Boost PIN Number is correct before sending or your transaction won't go through

PAY(space)AMOUNT(space)LAST FOUR DIGITS of your registered credit/debit card to add funds to your account using the credit or debit card you have already setup. Example PAY 58.95 1234 to add $58.95 to your account with your card ending in 1234. Or, Pay 50 1234 to add $50.00 to your account using card ending in 1234. Please don't enter a dollar sign- you don't need it. You only need to enter a 4 digit amount with a period between the dollars and cents for uneven dollar amounts. For even amounts (no cents) you only need to enter 2 digits. e.g. 50 for $50, not 50.00. Please note that you must reply with "Y" to confirm payment. Any other response will cancel the transaction. To setup a card go to boostmobile.com -> My Account.

Security Information:
For your security we recommend you delete the text messages after viewing, just in case you were to lose or have your phone stolen. We do not provide personal information with our text messaging service. For example, only the last 4 digits of your stored debit or credit card are sent by you to confirm payment. We also recommend password protecting your device to help prevent anyone else from accessing any information on your cell phone and is a convenient way to keep all your information on your device safe.
