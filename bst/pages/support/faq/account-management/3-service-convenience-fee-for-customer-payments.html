(top)

What is the $3 convenience fee?

Boost Mobile provides our customers with a variety of ways to Re-Boost for free, but if you need help with getting your payment made, we’re here to help! We know you want the best value in wireless so to try to give you that value, we’ll charge you a small $3 fee when you need us to help.

(top)

When can I expect to see the $3 service convenience fee?

When you choose to get help with your payments in a Boost Mobile store or when speaking to a Care agent, we’ll add the $3 convenience fee to your payment.

(top)

Do I always pay the service convenience fee?

There are several easy ways to avoid the fee:

  • You can enroll in Auto Re-Boost
  • Click the Boost Zone link on your Boost phone
  • Make a payment on boostmobile.com
  • Use Boost Mobile Wallet
  • Dial 233 from your Boost Mobile phone

Also, no fee is charged in a Boost Mobile store when you:

  • Pay your first activation payment on an account
  • Pay on a Boost Daily Unlimited or Pay-As-You-Go plans
  • Pay for an Add-On, like an international minute pack, before your monthly payment date
(top)

Do I have to pay taxes on the service convenience fee?

Yes, the local and state tax requirements will apply.
