What is Boost® Dealz?

Boost Dealz is a new service that enables you to get a $5 account credit every 30 days while viewing customized content and offers. It is a free app that runs in the background of your phone and that displays offers at various times when you swipe the lock screen on your phone before using it. The Boost Dealz app must be active on your phone for 30 days to receive the $5 account credit.

(top)

Is Boost Dealz available for all Boost Mobile plans and phones?

Boost Dealz is available for Boost Mobile Android smartphone monthly plans and Android phones with Android 4.0 and above. Currently, it is not available for daily plans nor iPhone, Blackberry, or Windows phones.

(top)

Is Boost Dealz compatible with an eligible Sprint phone that has been activated with Boost Mobile?

Yes, as long as the Sprint phone has Android 4.0 or above and is on a Boost Mobile monthly plan.

(top)

How much does Boost Dealz cost?

Boost Dealz is free to use and rewards you with a $5 account credit every 30 days as long as the Boost Dealz app is active during that period.

(top)

What are the content and offers displayed by Boost Dealz?

The content and offers are displayed at various times when you unlock your phone use. You can tell us what your interests are so we can deliver a more tailored experience for you.

(top)

Are the content and offers displayed in English or Spanish language?

Currently the content and offers displayed are in English language only. We are working on including content and offers that will be displayed in Spanish when the language preference on your phone has been set to “Spanish”.

(top)

Is Boost Dealz an add-on? How do I subscribe to it?

Boost Dealz is NOT an add-on. It is a free app that can be downloaded from the Google Play Store (just search “Boost Dealz”) or via Boost Zone. The first time you launch the app, you will be asked to sign-up with your Facebook account or with your email address.

(top)

How do I unsubscribe from Boost Dealz?

You can unsubscribe from Boost Dealz by selecting “Pause content and offers” in the “Profile” section within the app. You can also simply uninstall the app. When you unsubscribe from Boost Dealz or uninstall the Boost Dealz app, you will stop receiving the $5 credit every 30 days.

(top)

Will Boost Dealz renew automatically every month?

Your Boost Dealz subscription is independent from your monthly plan and add-on subscription. You will remain subscribed to Boost Dealz and will receive a $5 account credit every 30 days as long as the app is installed and active on your phone during that period.

(top)

Does Boost Dealz use my Boost Mobile high speed data allotment?

Yes, like use of any other app, watching content and offers through Boost Dealz will count towards your plan’s monthly high-speed data allotment.

(top)

What happens when my high speed data allotment is exhausted? Can I still continue watching Boost Dealz content and offers?

Yes, you will be able to watch Boost Dealz content and offers after your high speed data allotment is exhausted.

(top)

Can I use the Boost Dealz app outside the US?

Yes, you can as long as you are connected to a wifi hotspot.

(top)

Can I save a content or offer and go back to it later? How do I do that?

Yes, you can save customized content and offers by clicking on the “Heart” icon when it appears on your screen. You can then access your favorites at a later date via the favorites tab within the Boost Dealz app.

(top)

If I need help, how can I get answers? Who should I call?

Most of the answers to your questions about Boost Dealz can be found on the FAQ’s available on boostmobile.com. If you cannot find an answer to your question, please call Boost Mobile Customer Care for additional assistance.
