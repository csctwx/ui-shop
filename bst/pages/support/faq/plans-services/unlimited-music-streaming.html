What is Unlimited Music Streaming?

Boost Mobile Unlimited plans with Growing Data now let you rock your music! When you listen to one of the music service providers we've partnered with, you can stream as much music as you want on your Boost Mobile smartphone without it counting against your high-speed data allotment.

Please note that some streaming partners may require paid subscription or assess other fees directly to you. Those fees are not part of the Boost Mobile Unlimited monthly rates and are your responsibility directly with your chosen music service provider.

(top)

How do I sign up for Unlimited Music Streaming?

Unlimited Music Streaming is included automatically when you enroll in our current $35, $45, and $60 Boost Mobile Unlimited with Growing Data plans. It's easy - just pick a plan and the unlimited music streaming with approved music providers starts immediately!

(top)

What if I'm on an older Boost Mobile plan and want Unlimited Music Streaming?

You may already be getting Unlimited Music Streaming! We automatically added it to our existing Boost Mobile Unlimited with Growing Data plans. If you have one of these plans, you don't need to do anything - Unlimited Music Streaming is now included. Simply download one - or more - of our streaming music partners' apps through Google Play or the iTunes App Store and begin listening!

If your plan isn't listed and you want to change plans, you can do so here:

  • $35/mo - Unlimited Data / Talk / Text with 2GB High-Speed 3G/4G Data + Data-free Music
  • $45/mo - Unlimited Data / Talk / Text with 5GB High-Speed 3G/4G Data + Data-free Music
  • $60/mo - Unlimited Data / Talk / Text with Unlimited High-Speed 3G/4G Data + Data-free Music

(top)

Are you going to add more streaming music partners to your list?

Yes! We are working to add as many music partners as we can. Who you would like to see us plug in next? Click here to let us know, and we'll do our best to add them to the lineup. Technical issues may prevent us from adding everyone, but please know we are working hard to add every legal music service provider available in the U.S.

Visit boostmobile.com/datafreemusic/ every so often to see if your favorite music provider has been added.

(top)

Do I need to do anything to get Unlimited Music Streaming from new streaming music partners as they're added?

Nope, you don't have to do anything! As long as you are on one of our current $35/$45/$60 Boost Mobile Unlimited plans with Growing Data, you can begin listening as soon as you see your favorite music provider has been added to our program list and it won't count against your high-speed data allotment. Check back here every so often to see if your favorite provider has been added.

(top)

What if a music partner also streams video or lets me download a song? Is non-audio content also free?

This offer only applies to audio streaming with approved music providers. Other content types, such as cloud streaming, song downloads, album art or other non-audio content - even if it's coming from one of the approved providers - is excluded from the offer and will count against your monthly high-speed data allotment.
