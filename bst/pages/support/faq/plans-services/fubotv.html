What is fuboTV?

fuboTV is a live TV streaming service that bundles sports and other popular enterntainment TV channels. You can stream fuboTV content via your Boost Mobile Android and iOS phone, computer, and TV via Internet connected streaming devices. It is provided by our partner, fuboTV. All Boost Mobile customers will be able to subscribe and pay for this service directly with our partner, fuboTV, not through their BoostMobile account.

(top)

Is fuboTV available for all Boost Mobile plans and devices?

Yes, fuboTV is available to all Boost Mobile customers with iOS or Android phone or tablet(4.0 and above).

(top)

Is fuboTV an add-on?

No, fuboTV is a separate service that is not tied to your Boost Mobile account.

What are the differences between fuboTV and boostTV™?

fuboTV and boostTV™ are both video streaming services, however, there are several main differences:

  • boostTV™ is provided by Boost Mobile as an add-on to monthly customers. fuboTV is provided directly by our partner fuboTV and requires a credit or debit card.
  • The channels included in boostTV packs and fuboTV packs are different (with the exception of beIN SPORTS channels which are included in both services).
  • boostTV includes free channels. fuboTV includes a first month free.
  • fuboTV is available via iPhones, Android phones and tablets, computers and TV's connected to the internet via a popular compatible digital streaming media player. boostTV is available via Boost Mobile Android phones.
  • fuboTV includes Cloud DVR and closed captioning features.

Is the fuboTV video quality good?

The video quality displayed with fuboTV depends on a variety of factors; in most instances, it will be optimal when you are located in an area with good network coverage(visit boostmobile.com/coverage to check coverage)or, when you connect through a WiFi signal. In most instances, the app launches within a few seconds and you can start watching video content. If you are not under these conditions, picture quality may be affected by a variety of factors such as your location, the bandwidth available through and/or speed of your WiFi/Internet connection.

Does streaming video via fuboTV use my Boost high speed data allotment?

Yes, watching fuboTV video content will use your plan's monthly high-speed data allotment when you stream from your Boost Mobile phone. However, when you are connected via WiFi, or streaming fuboTV via your computer or on your TV via a compatible streaming media player, your high speed data allotment will not be used.

(top)

What happens when my high speed data allotment is exhausted? Can I still continue watching fuboTV?

After your applicable monthly plan data allotment is reached, access to data continues but data speeds (including video) will be reduced to 2G speeds for the remainder of your plan cycle. Speeds will be restored at the beginning of your next monthly plan cycle. You will still be able to continue watching fuboTV via WiFi without any limitations. You can upgrade your Boost Mobile plan to get a larger data allotment to continue watching fuboTV on the go.
