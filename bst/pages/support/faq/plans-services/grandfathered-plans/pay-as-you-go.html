Why is the Pay As You Go plan no longer available?

The Pay As You Go plans have been retired in an effort to create efficiencies within our plan portfolio. We've found that most customers who prefer a prepaid or no-contract offering actually prefer a monthly unlimited plan. In fact, our Monthly Unlimited Select plans offer the best value for most customers and remove the concerns about per minute rates.

With entry level price points as low as $35 a month or $2 a day for Daily Unlimited, we feel our portfolio is competitive and flexible to address the needs of all subscribers.

(top)

I am a current Pay As You Go customer, can I stay on this plan or do I have to switch?

You can stay on the Pay As You Go plan if it continues to suit your needs. Just be sure to keep your account active if you want to stay on this plan. If you change plans or if your account is canceled, you will not be able to change back to the Pay As You Go plan.

(top)

Can I change to another Boost price plan and then come back to Pay As You Go?

No. If you are a current Pay As You Go customer and change to another Boost price plan, you will not be able to change back to Pay As You Go.

(top)

What happens if I get a new phone?

If you get a new phone that is compatible with this plan, you can remain on the Pay As You Go plan. However, if you get any phone that is not compatible, (for example: an Android device or a Blackberry) you will need to select another plan that is available with your new phone.

(top)

When do I need to pay?

You will need to add money to your account at least every 90 days and you will need to add money to your account anytime your account balance reaches $0.

(top)

What if I do not add money to my account?

If you do not add money to your account at least once every 90 days, you will lose any balance on your account and your services will be interrupted. Anytime you reach a $0 balance, you have 60 days to add money to your account before your account is canceled.

(top)

What if my account balance reaches $0?

Once your account balance reaches $0, your services are interrupted (except calls to 911 and Boost® Customer Care). You will have 60 days from that time to add money to your account or your account will be canceled, you will lose the ability to retain your grandfathered Pay As You Go plan and you will lose your personal telephone number.

(top)

How much money do I need to have in my account?

You will need to have enough money in your account to cover the services you'd like to use. Anytime you reach a $0 balance, you will have 60 days to add money to your account before your account is canceled.

(top)

How much are calls to my voicemail?

If you use your Boost Mobile® phone to access your voicemail your call will be rated like any other call you make, just 20 cents/minute.
