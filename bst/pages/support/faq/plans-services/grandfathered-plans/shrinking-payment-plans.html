What is Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments?

Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments was a plan that Boost no longer offers for new customers. It included the benefit of Shrinking Payments that will allow you to see your monthly payment amount "shrink" $5 every time you make 6 on-time payments, up to $15/month in total.

(top)

What does Shrinking Payments really mean?

It means that your monthly payment amount will drop or "shrink" $5 each time you make 6 on-time payments, up to $15/month in total. You will still have all of the great features already included in the Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments plan including unlimited nationwide talk, text and data.

(top)

How can I get Shrinking Payments? What do I have to do?

Beginning July 1, 2014, Boost will no longer offer plans with Shrinking Payments to new customers. However, current customers can keep Shrinking Payments for as long as you keep your Boost account active and remain on a Shrinking Payments plan. Your Shrinking Payments rules and benefits will continue as usual.

If you decide to change your plan, or your account cancels, you will not be able to resume your former plan with Shrinking Payments.

(top)

What do I have to do to shrink my payments? And what do I have to do to keep my Shrinking Payments plan?

Each successful monthly payment made on your monthly payment date will get you closer to the next savings milestone. A great way to make sure you make your payment on time is by signing up for Auto Re-Boost

Current customers can keep Shrinking Payments for as long as you keep your Boost account active. If your account cancels or if you change plans, you will not be able to resume your former plan with Shrinking Payments.

(top)

What were the differences among the plans?

  • $45 Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments: was available exclusively for the BlackBerry® Curve™ 9310. Included unlimited talk, text and BlackBerry Messenger with BBM Voice. With no data services included, users could access data through a Wi-Fi Connection, where available.
  • $50 Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments: was available for basic/feature phones and included unlimited talk, text and 2.5GB/month of high-speed data.
  • $55 Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments: was available for smartphones and included unlimited talk, text and 2.5GB/month of high-speed data.
  • $60 Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments: was available for BlackBerry phones and included unlimited talk, text, BlackBerry Messenger and 2.5GB/month of high-speed data.

(top)

Can I use my device on another Boost plan?

The majority of Boost phones are eligible, except the BlackBerry 8330. Basic phones (non-smartphones) are only eligible for the $35 Monthly Unlimited or the $45 Monthly Unlimited plans.

(top)

On the $45 Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments plan, will I be able to send/receive email or IM, or surf the web if I am not using Wi-Fi?

No. The $45 Monthly Unlimited plan does not include data services unless you are connected to Wi-Fi. However, the $45 Monthly Unlimited plan does include unlimited use of BlackBerry Messenger.

(top)

Are 411 calls included with my BBM Unlimited plan?

No. 411 is not included but is available for $1.29 per call.
