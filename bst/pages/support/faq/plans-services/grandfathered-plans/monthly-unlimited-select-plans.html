What features are included in the Monthly Unlimited Select plans?

All plans include unlimited domestic talk, text, data (applicable 3G/4G high-speed data allotment) & 411 Directory Assistance calls (interactive voice services).

Additional services such as digital purchases (e.g., ringtones, games, wallpapers), international calls/messaging, add-ons, incidentals and premium messaging services (e.g., Hookt) are not included in the Monthly Unlimited Select plans and will incur additional charges.

However, current customers can keep their Monthly Unlimited Select plans for as long as they keep their Boost account active. Their plan terms and benefits will continue as usual. If their account cancels, they will not be able to resume their former Monthly Unlimited Select plan.

Do the Monthly Unlimited Select plans include Shrinking Payments?

No, the Monthly Unlimited Select plans do not include shrinking payments. The plans are based on your data needs so you can choose the price and data amount that is right for you. If you previously were on a Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments plan and decide to switch back, your previous savings amount and on-time payments will continue.

What phones can I use on these Monthly Unlimited Select plans?

Nearly all Boost phones currently available are eligible, except the BlackBerry 8330.

(top)

When is my monthly payment date?

Your monthly payment date is the same day of the month you made your first monthly payment. For example, if you paid your first month of service on February 15, then your monthly payment date is the 15th every month (March 15, April 15, etc.) and remains the same as long as you successfully make your monthly payment, on-time, every month.

If your monthly payment date is the 28th, 29th, 30th or 31st of a month then it will always be the same day or the last day of the month.

(top)

Can I change my monthly payment date?

No, your monthly payment date remains the same every month as long as you successfully make your monthly payment on-time.

(top)

What happens if I miss my monthly payment?

We’ve got your back here!  If you miss a payment, we will allow you to receive free domestic calls and SMS (text messages) for 60 days from your last payment due date.  Your account cannot be cancelled and you must have paid for at least one month of service in order to receive the free calls and texts. Until you pay for your service, you will not be able to make any outbound calls, texts or use data services.  (Learn more here).  Your outbound service will be interrupted until you add enough money to your account to cover your monthly payment amount. Once service is restored, your monthly payment date will change to one day prior to the date that service was restored (e.g., if your service is restored on February 15th, your monthly payment date will be reset to March 14th). Restoring service at the end of or on the first of the month (i.e., 30th, 31st or 1st) will result in varied payment dates contingent on the last calendar day of the month.

You have 60 days to resume service by paying your monthly payment in full or your account will be cancelled and you will lose all funds in your account balance and your phone number.

(top)

What happens if my account balance goes to $0 before my next payment is due?

If your account balance reaches zero, but you've already paid your monthly payment amount, you will still be able to use all the unlimited services included in your plan. However, you will not be able to purchase or use additional services (e.g., international calls). Please add extra money to your account to pay for these additional services.

(top)

Which plan is best for me?

If you rarely browse the Internet on your phone or primarily use Wi-Fi, the $40 Monthly Unlimited Select plan with 500MB of high-speed data may be right for you. If you listen to Pandora, watch YouTube, and regularly use the Internet, you should consider the $50 Monthly Unlimited Select plan with 2.5GB of high-speed data. If you're a heavy data user, our $60 Monthly Unlimited Select plan with 5GB of high-speed data is the best choice for you.

(top)

What happens when my data speeds are reduced?

After your applicable monthly plan data allotment is reached, access to data continues but data speeds will be reduced to 2G speeds for remainder of your plan cycle. Speeds will be restored at beginning of your next monthly cycle.

(top)

What happens if my data speeds are reduced and I want another high speed data allotment?

You need to wait until your next monthly payment date to get your high-speed data allotment renewed or you can use the Restart Plan option and make your full monthly payment early to re-enable all your services immediately by visiting My Account or calling Customer Care.

(top)

If I purchase the $40 Monthly Unlimited Select plan with 500MB of data and exceed the allotment, can I switch to a higher allotment plan?

Yes, you can switch to one of the higher allotment monthly plan plans and your new plan will be effective at your next monthly payment date.

How can I see and manage my data usage?

You can view your data threshold, usage and status in My Account. You will also receive notifications as you get closer to your included data allotment.
