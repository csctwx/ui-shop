Why is the $15 Mexico Mobile plan no longer available?

The $15 Mexico Mobile plan was available for a limited time.

(top)

I am a current $15 Mexico Mobile customer, can I stay on this plan?

You can stay on the $15 Mexico Mobile plan if it continues to suit your needs. Just be sure to keep your account active if you want to stay on this plan. If you change plans or if your account is canceled, you will not be able to change back to the $15 Mexico Mobile plan.

(top)

Can I change to another Boost International Connect plan and then come back to $15 Mexico Mobile plan?

No. If you are a current $15 Mexico Mobile customer and change to another Boost International Connect plan, you will not be able to change back to $15 Mexico Mobile plan.

(top)

Does the 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on include International Calls?

Yes, the 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on continues to include international calls to select countries and cities. Click here for International Calling Rates to all other countries and cities.

(top)

Does the 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on include unlimited calling to Mexico?

The $15 International Connect - Mexico Mobile add-on continues to include unlimited calling to landlines only in all of Mexico.

However, calls to mobile phones in Mexico are now limited to 1,000 minutes per monthly plan cycle, with each additional minute thereafter charged at $0.05 a minute.

(top)

What happens if I exceed my 1,000 minutes of calling to mobile devices in Mexico?

Once you use your entire 1,000 minute allotment to mobile phones in Mexico, you will be charged a rate of $0.05 for every minute after for the remainder of your monthly plan cycle. Any additional calling charges incurred will be deducted from available funds in your Boost Mobile account balance immediately. If there are insufficient funds available to cover the costs of the additional calling minutes, calls to mobile devices in Mexico will be blocked until sufficient money is added to the account balance to cover the incurred charges, OR until the next successful monthly payment is completed.

(top)

Why is Boost making this change to $15 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on?

Due to unexpected international settlement fee increases to all carriers, domestic carrier rates are adversely impacted as a result. Boost has sought to maintain a competitive international calling product despite this change, and is moving to a new model that still represents a competitive advantage in the 'calling to Mexico' space.

1,000 calling minutes to mobiles in Mexico. Afterwards, additional minutes used are only $.05 / min - a great value and one of the best rates in the industry.

(top)

How can I track my usage?

If you ever reach 75% (750 minutes) of your monthly allotment, you'll receive a text message warning notification. In February 2014, you will be able to track your usage online in My Account.

(top)

How do I sign up for the 'Mexico Mobile' International Connect add-on?

If you are not a customer yet, you can sign up when you activate your account, and you can begin using as soon as your account is activated.

If you are a monthly plan customer, you can sign up for $15 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on anytime during the month via My Account and the add-on will become effective on your next monthly payment date; you will be able to access all the included services after you have successfully paid your monthly payment amount including $15 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect add-on.

(top)

Where can I use the included services?

You can use the included services from the Boost domestic coverage area. See coverage maps for details.

(top)

When do I need to pay?

You must pay for 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect at the same time you pay your monthly payment amount, on your Monthly Payment Date. If your account balance cannot cover both the cost of your Monthly Plan and 'Mobile Mexico' International Connect on your Monthly Payment Date, your service will be interrupted.

(top)

If I have not subscribed to International Connect, can I still make international calls and send international text messages?

Yes. You will be charged accordingly on a per-minute and per-message basis. Check International Rates often for the latest details and rates for international calls.
