What are the benefits of enrolling in a Boost Family Plan over a Boost Single Line plan?

That’s easy – Family Plans offer flexibility, additional savings, and convenience!

Flexibility – Mix and match a Family Plan in a way that suits each individual needs of the family. Now, each individual can pick the plan with the amount of high-speed data that works best for them - some may want more high-speed data, some may want less.

Savings – Boost offers more savings by combining individual plans into one Family Plan. The more lines that are added, the greater the discount. Use the Savings Plan Calculator to determine your Family Plan discount and monthly payment amount.

Convenience – What’s more convenient than one monthly payment for all members of a Boost Family Plan? Plus Boost Family Plans have all of the features found on our single line plans:

  • Unlimited Data, Talk & Text, with different allotments of high-speed data for each plan
  • Domestic voice roaming (up to 50 minutes per month) extending your voice coverage area to 99% coverage nationwide
  • Unlimited Music Streaming without data charges (select partners)
  • Ability to turn your phone into a Wi-Fi Hotspot (data usage is drawn from your plan’s high-speed data allotment on the $35/$45 plans and a maximum 8GB of high-speed data on the $60 plan.)
  • Free incoming calls and text messages even if you have not paid; for up to 60 days
  • $5 Auto Re-BoostSM discount on the primary line of the Family Plan if registered for Auto Re-BoostSM.


Note: Our Family plans do not include Growing Data or Grace Period.

Boost’s Single Line Plans offer the added value of Growing Data in addition to the plan features above.

Can I get the discount benefit of the $5 Auto Re-BoostSM on Family Plans?

Yes, Boost offers a $5 discount only for the primary account holder of the Family Plan who registers for Auto Re-BoostSM. The discount will not apply to any secondary lines. Important Note: if the primary is enrolling for Auto Re-Boost for the first time, the discount will take effect on the next monthly payment.

What happens if someone leaves the family?

It depends on who leaves:

If the primary account holder leaves, the service of all the secondary lines will continue uninterrupted until the next monthly payment due date for the family. At that time a secondary line may want to either takeover as the primary line for the family and assume the payment responsibility for all lines, or make a plan change to a Single Line Plan. Either way, payment on the plan will be required by each secondary subscribers in order for service to continue the next month. In order to ensure your service is uninterrupted, we suggest you schedule a plan change for your next monthly payment date in My Account so you get the full benefit of services already been paid. Boost will not be able to transfer any unused account balance of the primary account holder to the secondary lines.

If a secondary account holder leaves, we will automatically recalculate the total monthly amount due by the primary account holder on the next monthly payment date. Since the Family Plan discount is based on the total number of lines in your family, the primary line will see a decrease in the Family Plan discount amount. We will adjust the next monthly payment amount to reflect the total monthly cost of the lines still in your family. Unused service will not be credited back to the primary line in this event. We think you will like the benefits of our Family Plan, but if you find yourself as a single payer, we suggest you consider switching back to one of our Single Line Plans with the added benefit of Growing Data for our $35/$45 plans.

Can someone make me be part of a Family Plan without my permission?

Your acceptance is required before you join a Family Plan, unless the primary account holder has your account PIN. We would encourage you not to give this information out unless it's to a trusted family or friend, and you can change your account PIN at any time in My Account.

(top)

Do all people joining a Family Plan need to join at the same time?

No. You can start, join or leave a Family Plan at any time. We will take care of prorating the monthly recurring charge based on the date of the action so that the primary account holder is not paying for a full month for members that are part of the family for only a partial month.

(top)

Am I going to get a credit for payments I already made into my Single Line Monthly Plan when I switch to a Family Plan? And how does it work?

It varies slightly based on each customer’s situation but this credit only applies to existing Boost customers who are joining an existing Family Plan group.

If you are new to Boost and creating a family group as the primary line, you will owe a full month’s payment for the Family Plan you select. Use the payment calculator to determine your full monthly payment for all members of your family group.

An existing Boost Single Line customer who is joining a Family Plan as a primary line, has a choice to change to a family plan immediately and lose any remaining airtime for that month. Another option is to set up the plan change to a Family plan to occur with the next monthly payment in My Account so you can use the remaining portion of their Single Line Plan service before paying for a new month on a Family Plan.

An existing Boost Single Line customer who is joining a Family Plan as a secondary line (not assuming the payment responsibility) AND has already paid for the current month, you will receive a prorated credit of unused service based on the date you paid the last MRC / 30, times multiplied by the number of days left until the primary line’s next monthly payment date. This will be applied to primary line’s account balance. We will align your monthly payment date to match the monthly payment date of the primary line at this time.

(top)

Can anyone in the family get an optional add on service, and who pays?

The primary line is responsible for paying for additional monthly recurring services for all lines in the family group including monthly recurring services purchased by individual secondary lines. One-time, non-recurring purchases are to be paid directly by the individual adding them to his/her account, and will not roll up to the primary account holder, unless he/she is the one adding them. None of these services are prorated if they are added mid-month.

(top)

Is data shared on a Family Plan?

No. Each individual uses their own allotment of high-speed data that comes with the unlimited data in their plan.

(top)

Is hotspot included in Family Plans?

Yes, just like our Single Line Plans, you can turn your phone into a Wi-Fi Hotspot. Data usage is drawn from your plan’s high-speed data allotment on the $35/$45 plans and a maximum of 8GB of high-speed data on the $60 plan.
