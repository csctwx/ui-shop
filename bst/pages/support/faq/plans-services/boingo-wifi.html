Estimating wireless coverage and signal strength is not an exact science. Who is Boingo?

Boingo provides WiFi access at over 1 million hotspots worldwide including airports, hotels, lounges, etc.

(top)

What is the Boost Mobile offer?

Sprint's partnership with Boingo allows for unlimited access to seamless and secure Boingo Wi-Fi networks in over 30 major U.S. airports at no extra charge.

(top)

Who is eligible?

New and existing Boost customers on any monthly or daily price plan and have a Boingo or partner WiFi capable device will have unlimited access to WiFi networks provided by Boingo at participating U.S. airports.

(top)

What devices are supported?

Click here for eligible devices. Devices require Sprint Connection Optimizer version 1.4.1641 or greater to be enabled.

(top)

How do I enable the Sprint Connection Optimizer on my device?

Select Settings, then Application then/ or Application Manager. Select "All Applications" at top right. Scroll to "Sprint Connections Optimizer" and select. The version is listed at the top of the screen under the title, "Sprint Connections Optimizer". If the application is Disabled, selecdt "Enable" at the upper right.

(top)

Does using the Boing hotspot service deplete my data allotment?

Data usage while connected to Boingo WiFi networks does not deplete data allotment provided within Boost monthly and daily plans.

(top)

What do I need to do to use a supported Boingo WiFi network?

Nothing! Your device will connect automatically as long as your Sprint Connections Optimizer is enabled or WiFi Passpoint (for Android 6.0 devices) is enabled.
