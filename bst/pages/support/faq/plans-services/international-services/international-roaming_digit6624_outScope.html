Important facts related to Mexico Travel Packs:

(top)

What is a Mexico Travel Pack?

When you subscribe to a Mexico Travel Pack you are able to use services while roaming in Mexico including receiving calls, making calls to destinations in Mexico and back to the U.S., sending international text messages (SMS only) and using the Internet. Select the Pack that best fits your calling and data needs when traveling to Mexico.

(top)

I already have an International Calling Add-On on my account. Can I add a Mexico Travel Pack, too?

Yes. The International Calling Add-Ons provide calling options from the US to other countries. The Mexico Travel Pack allows you to take your device to Mexico and roam on the cellular network. You can have both services on your account at the same time.

(top)

What countries can I call or text while roaming in Mexico?

With a Mexico Travel Pack you can make calls to the US or to destinations in Mexico. You can receive SMS messages and send SMS messages to the US and Mexico at no additional charge. SMS messages to other international countries will incur the international rate of $0.10/message.

(top)

What is the cellular coverage in Mexico?

Boost roaming coverage in Mexico is provided by Telefonica. For more details about coverage, see Mexico coverage.

(top)

Can I call Emergency Services in Mexico if I have a Mexico Travel Pack?

Yes, as long as you have purchased a Mexico Travel Pack, you can call local Mexico Emergency Services (066) while traveling in Mexico. You will receive emergency service in Spanish at your Mexico location. Emergency service calls will not incur any access fee or roaming charges.

(top)

What happens if I use all of my minutes or data in my Mexico Travel Pack?

If you use your voice or data allotment you will no longer be able make calls or access the internet. You can see how many roaming minutes and how much roaming data you have left through My Account on your device. Even if you run out of minutes and/or data during a visit to Mexico, you will still be able to send and receive text messages.

(top)

Are Mexico Travel Packs limited to certain devices?

Yes. Compatible Mexico Travel Pack devices support LTE/GSM technology. Please visit www.boostmobile.com or your local retailer for additional information.

(top)

What do I have to do to enable my compatible device to roam in Mexico?

When you add a Mexico Travel Pack on your account and you are currently located in the US, your handset will automatically update with the programming required to enable roaming in Mexico.

If you are already in Mexico and opt to add a Mexico Travel Pack you will need to connect your device to a WiFi network to access your My Account and then add the service. You can also choose to use another phone to call Boost Customer Care and have an advocate assist you. Once the pack is added to your account, you will need to update your handset's profile to receive all required programming for roaming. Follow the steps below for each device type.

Android

  1. Connect to Wi-Fi.
  2. Go to Settings > System Updates > Update Profile.
  3. Upon successful profile update the device may reboot.
  4. Go to Settings > System Updates > PRL Update.
  5. Go to Settings > Mobile Networks > Data Roaming > Roaming Network. Set to Automatic.
  6. Go to Settings > Mobile Networks > Data Roaming > International. Turn on Voice and Data.

iPhone

  1. Connect to Wi-Fi.
  2. Go to Settings > General > About.
  3. Click 'Carrier' to check the PRL version.
  4. Go to dialer, enter ##UPDATE# (##873283#), and click dial (green phone icon).
  5. When you see 'Service Update Complete,' click 'OK.'
  6. Go to Settings > Cellular > Roaming.
  7. Enable Voice Roaming.
  8. Enable Data Roaming.

Note that it is strongly advised that you add the Mexico Travel Pack to your account while you are in the US to ensure the updates reach your device properly.

(top)

What happens if I travel outside the US or Mexico?

When traveling outside of the US or Mexico coverage area, your wireless capabilities will be limited to Wi-Fi service only.

(top)

Important facts related to Mexico Travel Packs:

  • Primary usage must occur in the United States. Calls, texts or data roaming while in Mexico cannot exceed 50% of total usage or service may be terminated.
  • Mexico roaming coverage and service is not available everywhere.
  • Mexico roaming is only available on compatible LTE/GSM devices.
  • Data speeds are dependent on the Telefonica data network.
