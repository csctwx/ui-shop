What does the International Connect Plus plan add-on include?

  • 200 talk minutes to call mobile phones in over 50 countries including the Dominican Republic! See List of Countries
  • Unlimited talk minutes to landlines and mobile phones in Mexico.
  • Unlimited talk minutes to landline phones in over 70 countries, including Dominican Republic, Canada, Brazil, Venezuela, Colombia and more. See List of Countries
  • Reduced mobile and landlines rates to more than 200 international destinations including Dominican Republic.
  • Unlimited International text messages to over 200 international destinations worldwide from the U.S., Puerto Rico and U.S. Virgin Islands.
  • Visit International Rates to check if your destination is included and/or the latest rates.
(top)

What happens if I exceed my 200 minutes of calling to mobile devices?

Once you use your 200 minute allotment to mobile phones, you will be charged per minute for every minute after for the remainder of your monthly plan cycle. (View International Rates here) Any additional calling charges incurred will be deducted from available funds in your Boost Mobile account balance immediately. If there are insufficient funds available to cover the costs of the additional calling minutes, to destinations not included as unlimited, will be blocked until sufficient money is added to the account balance to cover the incurred charges, OR until the next successful monthly payment is completed.

(top)

How can I track my usage of mobile minutes?

You will receive a text message notification once you reach 75%, 90% and 100% of your 200 monthly mobile minute allotment. As an alternative self-service method, you may also track your usage online in My Account.

(top)

Can I receive calls from another country while in the U.S.?

Yes. There are no additional charges for receiving international calls.

(top)

How do I sign up for the International Connect Plus Add-On?

The $10 International Connect Add-On is only available for Boost monthly plans. If you are a Boost monthly plan customer, you can sign up for the $10 International Connect Plus Add-On any time during the month via My Account or your local, authorized dealer.

REMEMBER, adding the Add-On will increase the TOTAL amount due for your monthly services (monthly plan cost + add-on cost). Also, this is a recurring plan add-on; you must pay for it each month and will remain attached to your monthly plan and account until you remove it.

(top)

Where can I use the included services?

You can use the included services from the Boost domestic coverage area. See coverage maps for details.

(top)

How and when do I need to pay for the Add-On?

The International Connect Add-On is a recurring monthly plan add-on, with the cost being added to the cost of your monthly plan services. The new total plan cost must be paid at the same time you pay your monthly plan services and is due on your monthly payment due date. Your account balance must cover both the cost of your monthly plan services AND the cost of the International Connect Plus Add-On (and other plan add-ons, if applicable) by your monthly payment due date; otherwise, your services will be interrupted until sufficient funds covering the total costs of your plan and additional services (i.e., applicable add-ons, if any), are added to your Account.

(top)

If I have not subscribed to International Connect Plus Add-On, can I still make international calls and send international text messages?

Yes. However, you will be charged accordingly on a per-minute and per-message basis. Check International Rates often for the latest details and rates for international calls.
