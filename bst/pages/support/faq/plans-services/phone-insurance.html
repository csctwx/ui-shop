What is Phone Insurance?

Without insurance, you may be required to pay FULL PRICE for a new phone, if a replacement is needed.

  • Insuring your phone provides you peace of mind by protecting your phone in the event of loss, theft, accidental damage, and out-of-warranty mechanical or electrical breakdown.
  • It's easy and convenient. Visit myphoneguardian.com/boostmobile to file a claim 24 hours a day, 7 days a week or call 1-844-534-3099.
  • Your replacement phone is typically shipped within the next business day, once your claim is approved!

For $7.00 per month you are covered if your phone is lost, stolen, accidentally damaged - even with liquid, or has an out-of-warranty mechanical or electrical breakdown. Boost Mobile Phone Insurance covers any standard accessories included with the original purchase of the technology. 

Upon filing a claim, you will have to pay a nonrefundable deductible of $20, $50, $100 or $175 per approved claim based on your phone. You are eligible for 2 approved claims within a 12-month period beginning on the date of the first replacement. There is a per claim limit of $1,500 inclusive of accessories. Visit myphoneguardian.com/boostmobile to look up your deductible.

How can I protect the information on my phone if it's lost or stolen?

Boost Mobile Phone Insurance customers with a smartphone have access to Gadget Guardian™ powered by Lookout®. The app, once activated, will allow you to issue commands to locate, lock or wipe your smartphone if it becomes lost or stolen. You can also sound an alarm, even if the phone is in silent or vibrate mode. Access the app by visiting myphoneguardian.com/boostmobile/Security. Availability and features may vary by device.

How do I enroll in Phone Insurance?

Phone Insurance is only available to customers on a monthly plan within 30 days of service activation. You can add it during activation or by visiting My Account after you have activated your device. If you sign up when you buy your new phone or device, your coverage will start immediately. If you enroll within 30 days after you purchase your device, coverage will begin at the start of your next billing cycle. Please note that your handset must be fully operational at the time of program activation in order to enroll.

(top)

How do I know if my phone is eligible for Phone Insurance?

Phone Insurance is available for new phones within 30 days of activation, for upgrades to a new phone within 30 days or upon an equipment swap to a new phone within 30 days.

(top)

How do I file a claim?

Visit myphoneguardian.com/boostmobile 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to file a claim with eSecuritel (See Provider Information in brochure). You may also call 1-844-534-3099 from 6AM - 11PM CST, 7 days a week. You should be the primary account holder on the account and report the claim within 30 days of the incident, unless otherwise required by state law. If the approved claim involves a damaged or malfunctioning phone, you will be provided with detailed information on how to return the phone. If the phone is not returned within 10 days after receiving your replacement, you will be charged an unrecovered equipment fee up to $200.

How long does it take to get a replacement phone once I file a claim?

Once your claim is approved, you have provided any necessary documentation, and have paid your deductible, your replacement phone will be shipped to an address of your choice. If the claim is approved, you have provided the necessary documents, and you have paid your deductible by 11pm CT Monday through Friday and Saturday 7:30pm CT, you may receive your device the next business day.

(top)

What if I don't have enough money in my account to pay for Phone Insurance?

If you don't have enough money on your account to pay for both your service plan and your Phone Insurance, your service will become interrupted until you successfully make a payment. That means you won't be able to use your phone and will not have Phone Insurance coverage until you make a payment that covers your monthly payment amount and your Phone Insurance premium.

For example, if you are a monthly plan customer who pays $45 a month and you also have Phone Insurance for $7 a month, your monthly payment is $52. If you don't have $52 in your account when your monthly payment date arrives, your service will be interrupted and you will not have Phone Insurance coverage.

(top)

How do I cancel Phone Insurance?

You can cancel Phone Insurance by visiting My Account at BoostMobile.com. If you cancel between monthly billing cycles, you will receive a prorated refund or credit for any unearned premium.
