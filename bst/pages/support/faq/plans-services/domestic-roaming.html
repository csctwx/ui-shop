What is Roaming?

Roaming service is offered as a convenience so you can gain access to networks when you are temporarily outside the nationwide Sprint network. Roaming provides coverage on partner networks.

Boost Mobile wants you to stay connected, even when you cross those invisible lines! Now, 50 minutes per month of domestic voice roaming is included at no extra cost on select Boost Mobile monthly plans. Check here for plan and device availability. Roaming in Mexico is also available for an additional $5 a month for select Boost Mobile monthly plans. Visit International Services for more details.

(top)

Do I need to do anything special to be able to roam on Boost?

You need to be on a Boost Mobile Monthly plan that includes roaming and you need to have a device that has been enabled by us to roam. If you have both of these, you don’t need to do anything to roam. It will happen automatically.

Please check this list of devices often as we are adding more and more each day.

(top)

How do I enable Mexico Roaming on my phone?

You need to be on a Boost Mobile Monthly plan that is eligible for Mexico roaming and you need to have a device that has been enabled by us to roam in Mexico. Visit the International Services page Services for details about the add-on and the list of compatible devices. If you are on a compatible plan and device, then you can add the $5 Todo Mexico Plus with Mexico Roaming add-on within My Account or through your local authorized dealer.

(top)

Can I call, text and surf the web when I’m roaming?

Select Boost Mobile monthly plans automatically include 50 minutes of domestic voice minutes while roaming each month. Domestic text (SMS) and data roaming is not provided; neither is international roaming.

As a note, 50 minutes is more than sufficient for the vast majority of our customers who temporarily find themselves in roaming territory. Once you use all 50 minutes, your ability to make calls when roaming will be shut off until your next monthly payment is made and 50 minutes starts again. But don’t worry! You still have unlimited voice (non-roaming) minutes, unlimited text and unlimited data for the reminder of the month based on your plan’s allotment.

If you subscribe to $5 Todo Mexico Plus with Mexico Roaming add-on you are able to use services while roaming in Mexico including receiving calls, making calls to destinations in Mexico and back to the U.S., sending international text messages (SMS only) and using the Internet.

(top)

How will I know I’m roaming?

When you are roaming your device will indicate that you are not on the Sprint network. The symbol for roaming is the same for all devices – it’s a triangle symbol next to the signal strength icon on the top of your device and it will appear while you are roaming.

If you subscribe to $5 Todo Mexico Plus with Mexico Roaming and you travel to Mexico, your device will display “Movistar” at the top of the display when it has connected to the Movistar network.

(top)

Can I tell how many domestic roaming minutes I’ve used?

If you exceed your monthly allocation of domestic roaming minutes, we will send you a free text message notifying you that your domestic roaming minutes have been used for the remainder of your billing cycle. You will receive access to 50 minutes of domestic roaming with the start of your next month billing cycle. You will not incur any overages for roaming.

(top)

What happens to my call if I’m roaming and I reach the 50th minute?

Roaming calls that are in progress will be allowed to complete even if they exceed the 50th minute. We will send you a free text message after you reach the 50th minute so you know you will not be able to make any more domestic roaming calls until your plan restarts for the next month. But don’t worry! You still have unlimited (non-roaming) voice minutes, text and data for the reminder of the month based on your plan.

(top)

Can I get more domestic roaming minutes?

No. When your next month plan restarts, you will be given a new bucket of 50 domestic voice roaming minutes to use.

(top)

Does roaming cover all of the United States now?

Currently, Sprint’s agreements with its roaming partners covers 99% of the United States for voice services. Check here for coverage.

(top)

What if I don’t want to roam. Can I disable it?

Yes, you will find an option under the device settings on your phone where you can disable roaming, if it’s a roaming capable device.

(top)

I enrolled in the $35/$45 Growing Data Plans or the $60 Unlimited plans before 3/3/16. How can I get the benefit of the free domestic voice roaming feature?

To take advantage of the roaming benefit, you can simply change plans to the same price point plan to get this feature. Visit My Account, Select ‘Change My Plan or Add-On’ and ‘With Next Change’ to get this feature when your new plan month begins. If you want this feature now, choose ‘Now’. If you choose this option, your monthly plan payment will be due immediately and your bill cycle will change.
