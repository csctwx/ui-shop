What are Full Track, Ringtone & Ringback Tone Credit Bundles?

Our bundle services allow for savings for purchases of Full Tracks, Ringtone & Ringback Tones.

Ringback Tones are currently available for just $5/month. This bundle offers 3 Ringback Tones as part of your subscription. You can also continue to select from the Full Track Credit Bundle offers 5 Full Tracks for $5 and Ringtone Credit Bundle includes 3 Ringtones for $5.

By simply signing up for a Credit Bundle, you can instantly save over 20% on your favorite Full Tracks, over 30% on Ringtones and over 25% on Ringback Tones! Your Credit Bundle will automatically renew each month. Visit My Account to manage your subscription.

(top)

How do I sign up for Credit Bundles?

Credit bundles are available for purchase only through the Boost Mobile Music Store app. You can purchase credit bundles by:

  • Clicking on any of the banners promoting the bundles, located throughout the Boost Mobile Music Store.
  • Android and Win8 phone users: Click top left corner of the screen to access the fly-out menu. You will find your subscription statuses in the 'Other' section. Click the 'Info' button for additional information on the 5 for $5 or 3 for $5 tabs to purchase it.
  • Basic phone/WAP users: Click on 'Settings' (bottom right corner of the screen), then click on 'Subscriptions'. You will find your subscription statuses there. Click the 'Info' button for additional information on the 3 for $5 tabs to purchase it.

Please note, new users must first subscribe to the FREE Ringback Tone trial to qualify for the Ringback Tone Credit Bundle.

(top)

How do I use my 5 for $5 (Full Tracks) or a 3 for $5 (Ringtones) Credit Bundle?

Once you have successfully subscribed to the Credit Bundle, you will see a "1 Credit" button, replacing the previously seen dollar price against every Full Track, Ringtone or Ringback Tone. You will continue to see "1 Credit" pricing until all your credits have been used. A free Full Track, Ringtone or Ringback Tone will not count against your credit totals, thus they will continue to display a $0.00 price. To check your outstanding credit status, simply access the subscription status in the fly-out menu (Android/Win8 phones) or Settings section (Feature phones/WAP phones).

  • Purchasing a credit bundle will immediately grant you 5 credits for a full track credit bundle or 3 credits for a ringback tone OR ringtone credit bundle. You may start using your credits upon purchase.
(top)

How long do my credits last?

All credits expire 1 month from the date your Credit Bundle is purchased or renewed.

(top)

How will I be billed for a 5 for $5 (Full Track) or a 3 for $5 (Ringtone OR Ringback Tone) Credit Bundle?

Your purchase will simply be added to your monthly carrier bill.

(top)

Do I need to sign up for a Credit Bundle every month?

No. Your 5 for $5 (Full Tracks) or 3 for $5 (Ringtones OR Ringback Tones) subscription will automatically renew each month.

(top)

Do I use up a credit if I download a free Full Track or free Ringtone?

No. Free Full Tracks, Ringback Tones or Ringtones do not count against the credits you own. A free Full Track or Ringtone would show up on the store displaying a $0.00 price.

(top)

Can I purchase multiple Credit Bundles?

You may only have 1 Credit Bundle active at any point of time.
