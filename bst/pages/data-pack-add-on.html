What is a Data Pack and how does it work?

A Data Pack is an add-on that allows you to increase the amount of high-speed 3G/4G LTE data you have access to on your monthly plan. They are available for purchase and expire after 30 days.

(top)

What phones and plans are eligible for a Data Pack?

A Data Pack is available for purchase on all monthly plans that have data enabled (except the $60 Unlimited plan) and all phones but are not supported on any Tablet or Wi-Fi Hotspot product.

(top)

How do I add a Data Pack to my monthly plan?

You can add a Data Pack like any other add-on by visiting My Account. Please remember your account must have sufficient funds to cover the cost of the add-on service. This offer is not recurring and you will need to re-purchase/add them to your plan each time they expire (i.e., 30-day period for Monthly offer, or data allotment reached) if you want to continue service after they expire.

(top)

When I add a data pack, does my base plan data or the data pack get used first?

The Data Pack allotment will be used first and then default to the plan data allotment.

For example, if you are on a monthly plan that includes 2GB of data and you add a 1GB Data Pack, the 1GB allotment included in the Data Pack is used first and then defaults to the 2GB of data included in the plan. You can only have one Data Pack at a time until it is depleted.

(top)

How do I view my Data Pack usage?

To view your total data usage go to My Account.

(top)

Will I get messaged when I'm about to deplete my Data Pack?

Yes, Boost Mobile will send you a text message if you are approaching your data threshold.

(top)

What happens when I use up my Data Pack?

If you still have data allotment left in the monthly plan, then the data usage will come out of the monthly plan allotment until depleted or expiration. You may repurchase a Data Pack through My Account.

(top)

Is Mobile Hotspot included in the Data Pack?

No, the Mobile Hotspot add-on can be purchased separately through My Account. You can have Mobile Hotspot and Data Pack add-ons on your account at the same time; however, the Data Pack allotment will not be used for Hotspot use.

(top)

Can I have a Data Pack in addition to other add-ons?

Yes, Data Pack will work regardless of what other add-ons you have on your account.
