What features are included in the Data Boost plans?

Boost Mobile Plans include unlimited domestic talk, text (including text, picture and video messaging), and data (at applicable 3G/4G high-speed data allotment). All Boost plans are on the Nationwide SprintÂ® network. In addition Boost Mobile monthly plans include Free Unlimited use of 411, Call Waiting, Voicemail and 3-Way Calling. Additional services for International calling, Data Packs, Hotspots) are available for purchase. There are no contracts with Boost Mobile - just pay in advance for your monthly service and use it!

(top)

What happens if my data speeds are still reduced and I want another high-speed data allotment?

You have options. You can:

  • Wait until your next monthly payment date to get your high-speed data allotment renewed.
  • Purchase a Data Pack to add extra high-speed data to monthly plan. Learn more about Data Packs or visit My Account to purchase.
  • Use the Restart Plan option and make your full monthly payment early to re-enable all your services immediately. Visit My Account to complete.
(top)

If I purchase the $35 Data Boost plan with 1GB of data and exceed the allotment, can I switch to a different plan that includes more high-speed data?

Yes, you can switch to any of the plans that offer more high-speed data and your new plan will be effective at the beginning of your next monthly plan cycle.

If you have a basic phone (non-smartphone), you can only switch to the $45 Data Boost plan with 5GB of high speed data.

(top)

If I am an existing Boost customer, can I switch to a Data Boost plan?

Beginning October 1, 2015, Boost will no longer offer Data Boost Plans to customers. Customers who are on one of the Data Boost Plans can remain on the plan for as long as they keep their account current or until you decide to switch plans.

(top)

How can I see and manage my data usage?

You can view your data threshold, usage and status in My Account. You will also receive notifications as you get closer to your included data allotment.

(top)

What add-ons will be available with the Data Boost plans?

Almost all add-ons (e.g., International Connect, International Minute Packs, Phone Insurance, etc.) are available with Data Boost plans. The $5 Todo Mexico Plus add-on is available on the $45 and $55 Data Boost Plans. All other plans are excluded.

(top)

What plans are eligible for the $5 Todo Mexico Plus add-on?

The $45 and $55 Data Boost plans are eligible. All other plans are excluded.

(top)

What phones can I use on these Data Boost plans?

The majority of Boost phones are eligible, except the BlackBerry 8330. Basic phones (non-smartphones) are only eligible for the $35 Data Boost or the $45 Data Boost plans.

(top)

I have a basic phone (non-smartphone) and currently have the $55 Data Boost Plan, will I get to keep it?

Yes, as long as you keep your account active. If you cancel your account you will not be able to resume with the $55 Data Boost plan.

At the moment, the $55 Data Boost plan will not be able for new basic (non-smartphone) activations.
