What is Boost Mobile Wallet powered by Wipit?

The Mobile Wallet from Boost Mobile, powered by Wipit is a convenient and secure mobile payment solution that lets you use financial services and pay with cash directly on your Boost Mobile Android smartphone.

To learn more about Mobile Wallet, visit www.wipit.me/boost.

Download the free app from the Google Play Store (search keyword: Boost Mobile Wallet)

(top)

What can I do with a Mobile Wallet account?

With your Mobile Wallet account, you can:

  • Load cash to your wallet account at participating Boost Mobile Authorized dealers.
  • Use financial services in the Mobile Wallet app:
  • Send Money with Ria: Transfer money to more than 135 countries nationwide
  • Bill Pay: Pay more than 3,500 billers nationwide
  • Mobile Top-Up: Re-Boost your Boost Mobile phone account or top-up international prepaid wireless accounts
  • Quick Check: Photograph and load checks to your account using your Android phone1
  • Wipit Account Transfer: Send cash instantly to other Mobile Wallet Plus accounts1
  • Get a personalized, reloadable Visa Prepaid card, linked to your account.1
(top)

How do I sign up?

You can sign up online or from the Mobile Wallet app.2,3

Signing up is easy and only takes a few minutes and some basic personal information.

Need additional help? Visit a participating Boost Mobile Authorized Dealer for help creating an account.

(top)

Which account type is right for me?

Boost Mobile Wallet offers two different account types to meet your financial service needs.

With a Mobile Wallet account you can:

  • Send Money with Ria
  • Bill Pay
  • Mobile Top-Up

With a Mobile Wallet Plus account you get everything that a Mobile Wallet account offers plus:

  • Reloadable Visa Prepaid card
  • Quick Check5
  • Wipit Account Transfers4
(top)

Do I need a credit card or bank account to create a Mobile Wallet account?

No, you do not need a credit card or bank account to create a Mobile Wallet account. The Mobile Wallet is a prepaid service that does not require credit checks.

(top)

How do I add money to my account?

There are multiple ways to add money to your account, they are all simple and easy.

  • Cash: Use the Mobile Wallet app to find the nearest participating Boost Mobile Authorized dealer to load cash
  • Checks1,5: Use the Mobile Wallet app to snap a photo of check and have it approved and loaded to your Mobile Wallet Plus account
  • Direct Deposit1: Load your account safely and conveniently with free direct deposit service for Mobile Wallet Plus accounts
(top)

Where can I use my reloadable Visa Prepaid card?

You can use your reloadable Visa Prepaid card anywhere that Visa debit is accepted in the United States. That includes restaurants, hotels, websites, and more.

Who is Wipit?

Wipit is a leading provider in mobile payments for cash-preferred consumers, making it easier than ever to use cash in mobile apps, websites and prepaid cards. The Mobile Wallet uses Wipit to make fast and secure cash payments from the convenience of your Boost Mobile smart phone and a reloadable Visa Prepaid card.

To learn more about the Mobile Wallet, please visit www.wipit.me/boost.
