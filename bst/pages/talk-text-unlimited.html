Why is the Talk & Text Unlimited plan no longer available?

The Talk & Text Unlimited plan was available for a limited time and only on select devices.

(top)

I am a current Talk & Text Unlimited customer, can I stay on this plan or do I have to switch plans to Monthly Unlimited with Shrinking Payments or a different price plan?

You can stay on the Talk & Text Unlimited plan if it continues to suit your needs. Just be sure to keep your account active if you want to stay on this plan. If you change plans or if your account is canceled, you will not be able to change back to the Talk & Text Unlimited plan.

(top)

Can I change to another Boost price plan and then come back to Talk & Text Unlimited?

No. If you are a current Talk & Text Unlimited customer and change to another Boost price plan, you will not be able to change back to Talk & Text Unlimited.

(top)

What's included in the Talk & Text Unlimited plan?

The plan offers unlimited talk and text messages with the benefits of Shrinking payments! The Talk & Text Unlimited plan includes unlimited nationwide calling, messaging (text, picture, audio and video), voicemail, long distance, call waiting & call forwarding starting at just $45 per month. Email and IM are each available at $0.99/day when used. (Note: IM is not accessible on the LG Rumor Reflex). 411 is available at $1.29/call. Telenav may be purchased for $2.99/day or $9.99/ 30 days.

(top)

What's not included in the Talk & Text Unlimited plan?

Web access and data are not included in the Talk & Text Unlimited plan. You will have access to Boost My Account.

Additional services such as digital purchases (e.g., ringtones, games, wallpapers), international calls/messaging, Directory Assistance calls, instant messaging (IM), email, add-ons, incidentals and premium messaging services (e.g., Hookt, Telenav, etc.) are not included in the Talk & Text Unlimited plan and will incur additional charges.

(top)

Can I use any Boost phone with the Talk & Text Unlimited plan?

No. The Talk & Text Unlimited plan is only available with the Kyocera Coast™, LG Rumor Reflex ™, Sanyo Innuendo ™, Samsung Array™, and Motorola Theory ™.

(top)

How can I get wireless web?

Wireless web is not included in the Talk & Text Unlimited plan. To get unlimited wireless data, switch to a Monthly Unlimited Select plan.

(top)

Does the Talk & Text Unlimited plan include Shrinking Payments?

Yes! For every 6 on-time payments, your payment shrinks by $5 per month, from $45/month to as low as $30/month.

(top)

What happens if I get a new phone?

If you get a new Kyocera Coast™, LG Rumor Reflex ™, Sanyo Innuendo ™, Samsung Array™, or Motorola Theory ™, you can remain on the Talk & Text Unlimited plan. However, if you get any phone other than the Kyocera Coast™, LG Rumor Reflex ™, Sanyo Innuendo ™, Samsung Array™, or Motorola Theory ™, you will need to select a plan that is available with your new phone.

(top)

What add-ons are available with the Talk & Text Unlimited plan?

Phone Insurance, Telenav, International Connect, and International Connect Plus are available with the Talk & Text Unlimited plan.

(top)

When is my monthly payment?

Your monthly payment date is the same day of the month you made your first monthly payment. For example if you paid your first month of service on February 15, then your monthly payment date is the 15th every month (March 15, April 15, etc.) and remains the same as long as you successfully make your monthly payment, on-time, every month.

If your monthly payment date is the 28th, 29th, 30th or 31st of a month then it will always be the same day or the last day of the month.

(top)

Can I change my monthly payment date?

No, your monthly payment date remains the same every month as long as you successfully make your monthly payment. However, if you make a late payment, your payment date will be reset to the day of the month before the date your service is restored (e.g., if your service is restored on February 15th, your monthly payment date will be reset to March 14th) except reactivations made at the end of or on the first of the month (i.e. 30th, 31st or 1st), which will result in varied payment dates contingent on the last calendar day of the month.

(top)

What happens if I miss my monthly payment?

Your service will be interrupted until you add enough money to your account to cover your monthly payment amount. Once you have added enough money to your account to cover your monthly payment amount, your service will be restored and your monthly payment date will be reset to the day of the month before the date your service is restored (e.g., if your service is restored on February 15th, your monthly payment date will be reset to March 14th) except reactivations made at the end of or on the first of the month (i.e., 30th, 31st or 1st), which will result in varied payment dates contingent on the last calendar day of the month.

In order to resume service, your payment must be made in full within 60 days of service interruption or your account will be cancelled. When your account is cancelled, you will lose all funds in your account balance as well as your phone number.
