What are Ringback Tones?

Ringback Tones is a monthly subscription that allows you to assign songs to your contacts so they hear a song instead of the traditional call tone when they call you.

(top)

How do I sign up for a Ringback Tone subscription?

In the Boost Mobile Music Store app, there is an "Info" button next to each Ringback Tone. Select "Info" and it will guide you through the subscription sign up process.

(top)

Is there a monthly charge for Ringback service?

Yes, there is a monthly subscription fee of $0.99. You may be eligible to receive a 1 Month FREE Trial of the Ringback subscription. After the end of the free month trial period, a monthly recurring charge of $0.99 will be automatically applied each month until you cancel and unsubscribe from the Ringback Tone service. Each Ringback Tone is $1.99 and is valid for 6 months.

Ringback Playlists are $3.49 and are valid for 1 month. Free trial users may also have access to a limited selection of FREE Ringback Tones.

Alternatively, you can also subscribe to the Ringback Tone Credit Bundle, which provides you with 3 Ringback Tones for just $5/month.

(top)

How do I unsubscribe from the Ringback service?

You may unsubscribe from the Ringback Tone service by selecting "Settings" then "Manage My Subscriptions" and then, "Unsubscribe". A confirmation screen will be provided. You may also unsubscribe by calling Boost Mobile Customer Care at 888 BOOST 4U.

(top)

Am I eligible for 1 Month FREE Trial of Ringback subscription?

You are eligible if:

  1. You are currently not subscribed to Ringback service
  2. You have not subscribed to Ringback Tone service within the previous 3 months
  3. You have not previously participated in 1 Month FREE trial of Ringback Subscription
(top)

I subscribed to Ringbacks but the service is not yet active. Why?

Ringback Subscriptions may take up to 24 hours to activate. You will be informed via SMS upon successful processing of your subscription.

(top)

I purchased a Ringback Tone but it is not playing when people call me. Why?

Purchasing a Ringback Tone adds it to your Ringback library. You must go to your Ringback library and set the tone to either play as default or for a specific caller. Setting a Ringback Tone as default will ensure the tone plays for all callers. If you have customized the Ringback Tone based on time of day, it will only play during the time period. If you have added the Ringback Tone to a Shuffle List, the tone will play randomly.

(top)

Do Ringback Tones expire?

Yes. Most Ringback Tones have a six month expiry. Promotional free Ringback Tones and Ringback Playlists have a one month expiry. At the end of the license period, Ringback Tones renew at the current purchase price for a subsequent six-month period.

(top)

How can I stop a Ringback from automatically renewing?

To manage your Ringback auto-renewal period, go to the Ringback library, select the specific Ringback Tone for which you wish to manage auto-renew and from the available options, select "Turn Auto-renew off".

(top)

Are Ringback Tones available on my phone?

If you can view Ringback Tones in Boost Mobile Music Store, it means they are available on your device. Boost Mobile Music Store will only display content that is compatible with your Boost device.

(top)

What is the Shuffle List for Ringback Tones?

The Shuffle List enables your callers to hear a random Ringback Tone from a selection of your purchased Ringback Tones that you have added to the Shuffle List. To add a Ringback to your Shuffle List, visit "Ringback Tones" under "Library" from the main menu.

(top)

What are Ringback Playlists?

Ringback Playlists contain a selection of Tones of a genre or theme which will rotate each time you get a call. Ringback Playlists cost $3.49 and are valid for 1 month and will auto-renew thereafter. The Tones included in the Playlists will be updated monthly to ensure they are up to date with the latest hits.

(top)

In the Ringback Tone library, what are the 'Evening' and 'Day' settings for?

You may set a Ringback Tone to play only during 'Day' hours or 'Evening' hours. A Ringback Tone set for 'Day' hours plays between 6 am - 6 pm Eastern Time and a Ringback Tone set for 'Evening' hours plays between 6 pm - 6 am Eastern Time. If you leave either the 'Day' or 'Evening' options blank, no tone will play during the selected period.

(top)

I tried downloading a Full Track/Album/Ringtone, but was interrupted by loss of service. How do I re-download?

During a loss of service, a download in progress will be stored as "pending download." You can re-start the download at no additional charge. As a reminder, Ringback Tones are not a downloaded service, there is no file downloaded to your phone as it is a network based service only.
